Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about StoryWeaver

How does real-time collaboration work?

Our real-time collaboration uses advanced web technologies to sync your document instantly as you type. All collaborators see changes in real-time, and you can track who's currently editing which part of the document.

Can I use StoryWeaver for non-fiction writing?

Absolutely! StoryWeaver is designed for all types of writing - novels, screenplays, academic papers, business documents, and more. Our platform adapts to your writing style and content needs.

How secure is my writing?

Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. All documents are stored in secure AWS facilities with regular security audits. You retain full ownership of your content.

Can I export my work in multiple formats?

Yes! You can export your documents as PDF, Word (.docx), or even directly to major publishing platforms. We also support exporting character and word counts for your submission needs.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Premium plan. No credit card required. You'll have full access to all Premium features during your trial period.
