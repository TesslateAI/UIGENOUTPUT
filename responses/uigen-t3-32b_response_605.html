Simplify Your Scheduling in 3 Easy Steps

Discover how 'Simplify Scheduling' makes connecting with others effortless and efficient.

Share Your Link

Generate a unique, shareable link to your personalized schedule. Send it via email, chat, or embed it on your website.

Choose a Time

Your clients or colleagues simply click your link and pick a convenient time slot from your available calendar.

Confirm & Join

Once a time is chosen, both parties receive a confirmation. You'll get a calendar invite, and they'll get a direct link to join your meeting.
