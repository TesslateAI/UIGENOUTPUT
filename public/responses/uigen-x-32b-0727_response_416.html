How does the corporate plan work?

Our corporate plans provide a weekly delivery of fresh, healthy snack boxes to your office. You choose the number of boxes and the plan type. We handle all ordering, delivery, and billing through our platform.

Can we customize the snacks?

Yes! Our Professional and Enterprise plans offer customization options. You can select specific snack types, accommodate dietary restrictions, and even add your company branding to the boxes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and ACH transfers. For Enterprise plans, we can also arrange invoice-based billing with net-30 payment terms.

How do we manage orders and preferences?

We provide a dedicated portal where you can manage your subscription, update preferences, view delivery history, and track spending. Our platform is designed for easy team management.
