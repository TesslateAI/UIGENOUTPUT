FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about the pre-release experience

What is the early access program?

The early access program allows a select group of players to experience the game before its official release. Participants receive special discounts, early access to content, and exclusive rewards.

How do I apply for early access?

We're accepting applications until we reach our early access cap. Check our website for the application form. We prioritize players who have supported us on social media, left reviews, or are part of our community Discord server.

What platforms will Nexus be available on?

Nexus will launch on PC (Windows 10/11) and PlayStation 5. We're also working on versions for Xbox Series X/S and Nintendo Switch, with support for all these platforms coming at the same time.

Can I play the demo on multiple platforms?

Yes! Your demo progress will sync across all platforms you own. We're working on cloud saved progress that will work across PC, PlayStation, and Xbox.
