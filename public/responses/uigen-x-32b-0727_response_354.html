Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How soon will I see results?

Most clients see noticeable changes within 4-6 weeks. The exact timeline depends on your starting point, goals, and consistency with the program.

Do I need to have experience to work with you?

Not at all! I work with clients of all fitness levels, from complete beginners to advanced athletes. Programs are tailored to your individual needs and abilities.

What if I need to cancel a session?

You can cancel up to 24 hours before your scheduled session without any charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, I offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the results or service, you can request a full refund within the first month.
