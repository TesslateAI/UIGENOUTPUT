Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recruitment process.

How long does the hiring process take?

Our average hiring process takes 2-4 weeks from resume submission to job offer. The timeline can vary based on the company and position, but we prioritize fast and efficient hiring.

Do you work with international candidates?

Yes, we work with candidates worldwide and have partnerships with tech companies in over 20 countries. We assist with relocation processes and visa support when applicable.

What types of IT positions do you specialize in?

We specialize in a wide range of IT positions including software development, DevOps, cloud computing, cybersecurity, data science, UX/UI design, and IT management roles.

Is there a cost to use your services?

No, our platform is completely free for job seekers. We generate revenue through fees paid by the companies that hire through our platform.
