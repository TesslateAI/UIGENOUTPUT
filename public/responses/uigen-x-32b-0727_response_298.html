Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the FutureFounders Accelerator.

What stage should my startup be in to apply?

We accept startups at the early-stage (pre-seed or seed) with a validated idea, a minimum viable product, and a clear vision. Your team should be formed but you don't need to have customers yet.

How much funding do you provide?

We invest $50,000 in each selected startup in exchange for 5-7% equity. In addition, we provide access to follow-on funding opportunities and our network of investors.

What industries do you focus on?

We invest in a wide range of technology sectors including AI, SaaS, fintech, healthtech, edtech, and clean tech. We're most excited about solutions that solve real problems with innovative approaches.

How long is the program and what's the time commitment?

The program is 12 weeks intensive, typically running from June to September. We expect founders to be fully committed during this period, with regular meetings and milestones.

Do you provide mentorship after the program ends?

Yes! Our alumni network and mentorship continue beyond the 12 weeks. We maintain strong relationships with all our portfolio companies and provide ongoing support as they scale.
