Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our therapy services

Is online therapy effective?

Yes, numerous studies have shown that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person sessions for many mental health concerns. Our therapists use evidence-based approaches that work well in a virtual setting.

How do I know if I need therapy?

Therapy can help with a wide range of concerns including stress, anxiety, depression, relationship issues, grief, and personal growth. If you're feeling overwhelmed or struggling to cope, it might be a good time to reach out.

How are sessions billed?

We offer flexible payment options including per-session fees and subscription plans. We accept most major credit cards and offer sliding scale options for those with financial needs.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are confidential and comply with HIPAA regulations. Our platform uses end-to-end encryption to ensure your privacy and security.

Can I choose my therapist?

Yes, you can browse our therapist profiles and select someone who feels like a good fit for you. If you're not completely comfortable after your first session, we'll help you connect with another therapist at no additional cost.
