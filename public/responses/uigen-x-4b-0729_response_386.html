Creamy Mushroom Risotto

45 min 4 servings

A rich and creamy Italian classic that's easy to make with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

1 cup
Arborio rice
4 cups
Vegetable broth
1 lb
Mushrooms
1/2 cup
Onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup
White wine
1/2 cup
Heavy cream

Instructions

1
Sauté onions

Cook onions in olive oil until translucent. Add mushrooms and cook until tender.

2
Toast rice

Stir in rice, cook for 2 minutes until slightly toasted. Remove from heat.

3
Deglaze with wine

Add white wine, stirring until absorbed. Then add broth 1/2 cup at a time, stirring constantly.

4
Finish with cream

Stir in cream and butter. Cook until creamy and al dente. Season to taste.
