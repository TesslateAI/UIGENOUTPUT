Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products

Is your food suitable for pets with allergies?

Yes, we offer limited ingredient diets specifically formulated for pets with food sensitivities. Our recipes are free from common allergens like corn, wheat, soy, and artificial additives.

How should I transition my pet to your food?

We recommend transitioning over 7-10 days. Start with 25% of the new food mixed with 75% of the current food, gradually increasing the proportion each day. This helps prevent digestive upset.

Where are your ingredients sourced from?

We source our ingredients from trusted suppliers in North America and Europe. Our meat comes from farms that follow ethical animal husbandry practices, and our produce is sourced from organic farms when possible.

How long does shipping take?

We ship orders within 1-2 business days. Most orders arrive within 3-5 business days. We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50.
