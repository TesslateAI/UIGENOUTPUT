Our Mission & Values

We're committed to creating a future that works for everyone through progressive policies and community engagement.

Community First

We believe in solutions that empower communities and prioritize the well-being of all citizens.

Economic Justice

Creating fair economic systems that provide opportunity and security for every worker.

Environmental Stewardship

Protecting our planet for future generations through sustainable policies and innovation.

Our Policy Platform

Comprehensive solutions to the challenges we face, designed to create lasting change.

Economic Reform

We'll implement fair wage policies, expand job training programs, and create a more equitable tax system to ensure everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

  • Universal basic income pilot program
  • Living wage standard for all workers
  • Tax incentives for small businesses

Healthcare for All

Access to quality healthcare is a fundamental right. We'll expand coverage and reduce costs for families.

  • Medicare expansion to cover all citizens
  • Reduce pharmaceutical prices
  • Invest in rural healthcare infrastructure

Climate Action

We'll transition to renewable energy, protect natural habitats, and create green jobs to combat climate change.

  • 100% renewable energy by 2035
  • Green infrastructure investment
  • Carbon tax to fund clean energy

Education Equity

Every child deserves a quality education. We'll invest in schools and make higher education affordable.

  • Universal pre-K program
  • Tuition-free community college
  • Stable funding for public schools
