Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our recruitment services.

How long does it take to get matched with a company?

Most candidates receive their first match within 1-3 business days. Our team reviews profiles quickly and connects you with relevant opportunities.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in software development, cloud computing, data science, cybersecurity, AI/ML, and DevOps. Our network covers all major tech sectors.

Do you offer free resume reviews?

Yes! We offer a free resume review service for all new candidates. Our experts will help you optimize your resume for tech roles.

What fees do you charge?

We charge a flat fee of $499 per position filled. This covers all recruitment services including screening, matching, and support throughout the process.
