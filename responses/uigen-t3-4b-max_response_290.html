Frequently Asked Questions

What is a vulnerability scan, and how does it work?

A vulnerability scan is an automated process that identifies weaknesses in your IT systems, such as outdated software, misconfigurations, or unpatched vulnerabilities. It helps us understand what could be exploited by cyber attackers.

Is the free scan truly no-obligation?

Absolutely! We offer a completely free and non-binding vulnerability scan. You can review the findings and decide if our services are right for your business without any commitment.
