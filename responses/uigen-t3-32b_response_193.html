Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our personalized language tutoring.

What languages do you offer tutoring for?

We offer tutoring for a wide range of languages, including Spanish, French, German, Mandarin Chinese, Japanese, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, and Arabic, among others. Let us know your target language!

How does personalized instruction work?

After a brief initial assessment, your tutor will create a customized learning plan based on your current level, goals, and preferred learning style. This ensures your lessons are always relevant and effective.

What are the trial lesson details?

Our free trial lesson is a 30-minute session where you can experience our teaching style and meet your tutor. It's a great way to see if LanguageLink is the right fit for you.

Can I cancel my trial lesson?

Yes, you can cancel your trial lesson up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. Please contact us via email or phone to make any changes.
