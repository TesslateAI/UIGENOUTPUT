Frequently Asked Questions

Is the platform free?

Yes, you can sign up for free and start learning! Advanced features are available for a small fee.

Can I learn multiple languages?

Absolutely! You can create multiple profiles to connect with native speakers of different languages.

How do I start a conversation?

Once you've found a partner, you can initiate a video call, chat, or share a language learning resource.

What if I have a language barrier?

Our platform is designed to be inclusive. We encourage patience, clear communication, and the use of translation tools if needed.
