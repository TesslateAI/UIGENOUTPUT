Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PixelPerfect Presets.

A Lightroom preset is a saved set of adjustments you can apply to your photos in Adobe Lightroom. It includes changes to exposure, contrast, saturation, clarity, and more, making it easier to achieve a consistent look.

Our PixelPerfect Presets are designed for Lightroom Classic. While they won't work directly in Lightroom mobile, they can be transferred to an iPad via the Lightroom mobile app. Always check the download instructions for the most accurate compatibility.

After purchasing, you will receive a download link. Follow the instructions provided to install the presets. This typically involves opening the downloaded file, and then importing them into your Lightroom preferences. Detailed steps are included in your download.

We provide comprehensive support via our contact form and email. For questions about the presets themselves, we'll do our best to help. For issues with installation or specific presets, please contact us.
