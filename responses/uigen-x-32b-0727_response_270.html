Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating your personalized book.

How long does it take to receive my book?

Once you complete your customization, it typically takes 5-7 business days to design, print, and ship your book. We use expedited shipping to get it to you as quickly as possible.

Can I see a preview before it's printed?

Yes! After you complete your customization, you'll receive a digital proof of your book. You can review the story, illustrations, and character designs. We make revisions until you're completely satisfied.

What age range are the books appropriate for?

Our books are designed for children aged 2-8 years old. We have different story templates and reading levels to match your child's age and reading ability. All stories are carefully crafted to be age-appropriate.

Can I include multiple children in one book?

Absolutely! Our Family Collection package allows you to include up to 3 children in one book. Each child can have their own character with custom names and appearances. We can also create stories that feature siblings or friends going on adventures together.
