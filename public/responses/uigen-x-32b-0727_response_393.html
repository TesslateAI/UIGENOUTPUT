About Our Community Garden

We believe in the power of community and the healing benefits of gardening. Our urban oasis brings neighbors together to grow, learn, and thrive.

Organic Gardening

We practice sustainable, chemical-free gardening methods to produce the healthiest produce for our community.

Biodiversity

Our garden is a haven for pollinators and wildlife, promoting ecological balance in our urban environment.

Community Focused

We provide a welcoming space for all skill levels to learn, share knowledge, and build meaningful connections.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our community garden membership.

No prior experience is required! We welcome beginners and provide training, tools, and support to help you succeed. Our experienced staff and community members are always available to assist.

Your membership includes access to your assigned garden plot, basic gardening tools, water, soil, compost, and our community knowledge base. Depending on your plan, you'll also receive weekly harvest shares and workshop access.

Yes! You have freedom to choose what you grow, though we provide guidance on seasonal crops best suited for our climate. We also maintain a community section for shared crops like corn and sunflowers.

We recommend spending 2-3 hours per week on your plot. Our community helps maintain the garden together, and we have a flexible plot sharing program for members with limited time.

We have a buddy system and community support to help maintain your plot temporarily. For extended absences, you can pause your membership or share your plot with another member through our sharing program.
