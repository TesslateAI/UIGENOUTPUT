Frequently Asked Questions

What age do I need to be?

We welcome users from 16 years and above to ensure a respectful and beneficial learning environment.

Is it free to use?

Yes, SpeakEasy is completely free to join and use. We offer premium features for those who want to expand their experience.

How do I know my partner is genuine?

We encourage users to carefully review each other's profiles, including their language proficiency and goals. We also have a reporting system in place.

What devices are supported?

SpeakEasy is fully responsive, so you can use it on desktops, tablets, and smartphones without any issues.
