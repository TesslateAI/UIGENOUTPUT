Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Artisan Aroma.

What does the Artisan Aroma box include?

Each box includes premium natural soy wax, essential oils for a specific scent, wicks, candle containers (mason jars or tins), a detailed instruction guide, and sometimes bonus crafting accessories.

How often are the boxes delivered?

Boxes are delivered every three months (quarterly) to your doorstep. You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time.

Are the scents truly natural?

Absolutely! We use only 100% natural essential oils, not synthetic fragrances, to provide authentic and high-quality scents that are gentle on your skin and home.

What if I'm a beginner?

Our kits are designed with beginners in mind. The detailed, step-by-step instructions make candle-making easy and fun for everyone, regardless of experience level.
