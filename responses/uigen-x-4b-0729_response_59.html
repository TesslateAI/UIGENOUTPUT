Our Story

Brew Haven was founded in 2008 by coffee enthusiast Maria Rodriguez. What started as a small coffee stand in her garage has grown into a beloved community coffee shop with a passion for specialty coffee and artisanal pastries.

We believe in slow, intentional coffee culture. Our beans are sourced directly from sustainable farms in Colombia and Guatemala, and our baristas are trained to bring out the best flavors in every brew.
