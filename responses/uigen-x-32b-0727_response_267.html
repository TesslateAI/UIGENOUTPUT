Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coffee subscription service.

How often will I receive my coffee?

You'll receive your coffee subscription on a monthly basis. After your initial order, your coffee will be delivered every 28 days. You can adjust your delivery schedule or pause your subscription at any time through your account.

Can I change or cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can pause, skip, or cancel your subscription at any time. Simply log into your account and adjust your subscription preferences. Changes take effect immediately, and you won't be charged for any skipped months.

How are your coffee beans roasted?

We roast our coffee in small batches using state-of-the-art equipment to ensure consistency and quality. Our roasting process is designed to highlight the unique flavor profile of each origin while preserving the bean's natural characteristics. Beans are roasted to order and shipped within 24 hours of roasting.

What brewing methods do you recommend?

Our coffee is versatile and works well with most brewing methods. However, we recommend pour-over or drip brewing to best experience the complex flavor profiles of our single-origin beans. Each shipment includes a brewing guide tailored to the current coffee's characteristics.

Do you offer gift subscriptions?

Absolutely! We offer gift subscriptions that make perfect presents for any occasion. When purchasing a gift subscription, you can include a personalized note and choose the delivery schedule. The recipient will receive their first shipment within 1-2 business days.
