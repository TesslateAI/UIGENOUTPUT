Eco-Friendly Materials

Our packaging is made from sustainable, renewable resources that minimize environmental impact.

Plant-Based Plastics

Derived from corn starch, sugarcane, or potato starch. Fully compostable under industrial conditions.

FSC-Certified Paper

Sourced from responsibly managed forests. Recyclable and often processed with low environmental impact.

Mushroom Packaging

Made from agricultural by-products and mushroom roots. 100% compostable and biodegradable.

Seaweed Film

Extracted from seaweed, this material is edible, compostable, and has excellent barrier properties.
