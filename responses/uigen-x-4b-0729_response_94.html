Quick Tip for Beginners

When starting your meditation practice, find a quiet space and sit comfortably. Close your eyes and focus on your natural breath. Don't worry about perfect form - just be present in the moment.

Start with 3-5 minutes daily

Breath Awareness

When you're feeling overwhelmed, try this simple technique: inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 2 seconds, and exhale for 6 seconds. Repeat for 5 minutes.
