Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our craft beer club membership.

How does the membership work?

When you join, you'll receive a curated selection of craft beers each month. You can choose to pause, skip, or cancel your membership at any time. Each delivery includes 4, 8, or 12 beers depending on your plan.

Can I choose which beers I receive?

Our monthly selections are curated by our expert panel to showcase the best craft beers available. While you can't choose specific beers, you can provide your preferences in our member portal to help us tailor future selections to your taste.

What if I don't like a beer I receive?

We want you to love every beer you receive! If you're not satisfied with any beer in your shipment, simply let us know within 14 days and we'll credit your account for the value of that beer. No questions asked.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to all 50 US states and Washington DC. We're working on expanding our international shipping to Canada and the UK in early 2024. Check our website for the latest updates.

Can I gift a membership to someone else?

Absolutely! We offer gift memberships for all three plans. You can purchase a gift membership for any length of time and we'll ship the first box directly to your recipient with a personalized message.
