Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InboxZero.

How does the AI sorting work?

Our AI analyzes your email patterns and content to automatically categorize messages. It learns over time to prioritize important items, identify spam, and suggest responses based on context and your past behavior.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for our Professional plan. No credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period.

Can I use InboxZero with multiple email accounts?

Yes, InboxZero supports multiple email accounts. You can connect all your email providers including Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, and more through our secure API integration.

What devices are supported?

InboxZero works on all major platforms including Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android. We also have a web version available at inboxzero.com.

How secure is my data?

We take security seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We don't store your emails or personal information on our servers without your explicit consent.
