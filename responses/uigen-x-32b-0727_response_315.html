Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coaching process

How long does the coaching process take?

Our standard program is 6 weeks, with weekly 90-minute sessions. Many clients see significant shifts in mindset and results within the first 2-3 sessions. The exact timeline depends on your specific goals and commitment.

What makes your coaching different?

We combine neuroscience-based mindset work with practical business strategy. Unlike generic coaching, we focus specifically on the unique challenges entrepreneurs face and provide actionable steps to implement mindset shifts in your business context.

Do you guarantee results?

We guarantee a shift in mindset and increased awareness. While we can't control external market factors, 95% of our clients report measurable business improvements within 3 months of completing our program. We offer a full refund if you don't feel any benefit after our first session.

Can I do this if I'm not currently in business?

Absolutely! Our coaching is for anyone with entrepreneurial aspirations or who wants to overcome limiting beliefs in their personal or professional life. Many of our clients are preparing to launch their business or transitioning into entrepreneurship.
