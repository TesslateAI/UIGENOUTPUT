About Our Charity Run

The Run for a Cause event is an annual 5K and 10K race designed to raise funds and awareness for underprivileged children in our community. It's more than just a race; it's a celebration of community spirit and a powerful way to create lasting change.

Event Details

  • Date: October 20, 2024
  • Location: Central Park, Cityville
  • Start Time: 8:00 AM (5K), 8:30 AM (10K)
  • Registration Fee: $35 (5K), $50 (10K)
  • Awards: Medals for all finishers, age group awards

Registration deadline: September 30, 2024. Don't miss out!
