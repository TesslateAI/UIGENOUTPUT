Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HomeSense

How easy is it to install the HomeSense devices?

Most devices require minimal installation. The smart lock takes about 15 minutes to install, while cameras and sensors can be mounted with adhesive strips. Our app provides step-by-step installation guides.

Do I need an internet connection for the system to work?

The system requires an internet connection for setup and remote access. However, all devices will still function locally during outages. Motion sensors and door sensors will trigger alarms and store events locally.

How long do the batteries last?

Motion sensors last up to 2 years on a single battery. The smart lock battery lasts about 1 year with average use. All devices have low battery indicators that notify you when it's time to replace batteries.

Can I add more devices later?

Absolutely! You can add additional cameras, sensors, and other smart home devices to your system at any time. Our starter kit includes everything you need to get started, but you can expand as your needs grow.
