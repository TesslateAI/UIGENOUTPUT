Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about online therapy

Yes, online therapy is just as effective as in-person sessions when conducted by a qualified professional. Research shows that it can be particularly beneficial for individuals who face barriers to traditional therapy, such as geographic location or mobility issues.

Our online therapy platform uses secure video conferencing technology to connect you with your therapist. You'll receive a unique link to access your session. The process is confidential and follows the same professional standards as in-person therapy.

We understand that everyone is different. If you feel more comfortable in person, we can arrange for you to meet at our office. We're here to help you find the most appropriate format for your needs.

Online therapy can help with a wide range of issues including depression, anxiety, relationship problems, grief, career transitions, stress management, and personal growth. Therapists are trained to address various concerns and can develop a personalized plan to meet your specific needs.

We provide detailed profiles of all our therapists, including their specialties, qualifications, and therapeutic approaches. You can also read client reviews and contact therapists directly to ask questions before booking sessions.
