Unleash Your Inner Space Hero

'Galaxy Raiders' is a high-octane, action-packed mobile game that combines fast-paced combat with strategic elements. Command your elite squad of intergalactic warriors and engage in thrilling battles against alien hordes across diverse and stunning alien worlds.

From ancient alien ruins to futuristic cyber-cities, explore vast, immersive environments filled with secrets, powerful weapons, and legendary artifacts. Team up with friends for cooperative missions or dominate the leaderboards in intense PvP arenas. The cosmos awaits your command!
