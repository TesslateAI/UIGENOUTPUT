Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about career coaching

How long does a career transition typically take?

The timeline varies based on individual circumstances, but most clients complete their transition within 3-9 months. Our Career Transition package provides a structured 6-week program to accelerate your progress.

Do you help with resume and job search?

Yes! Our Career Transition and Ongoing Support packages include comprehensive resume and LinkedIn optimization, as well as job search strategy and interview preparation.

What if I'm not sure what I want to do next?

That's perfectly okay! Our Clarity Session is designed specifically for individuals who are feeling stuck or uncertain. We'll help you explore options and discover your ideal path.

Can I get a refund if coaching isn't the right fit?

Yes, we offer a full refund for the Clarity Session if you're not satisfied. For our coaching packages, we offer a prorated refund for unused sessions after the first two meetings.
