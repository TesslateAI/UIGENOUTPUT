Energy Saving Tips

Optimize AC Usage

Set thermostat to 78°F when at home and 85°F when away to save up to 15% on cooling costs.

Unplug Devices

Electronics consume power even when turned off. Unplug devices not in use or use smart power strips.

Shorten Showers

Reduce shower time by 1-2 minutes to save water and energy used to heat it.

Use Natural Light

Open curtains during the day to reduce lighting needs and use task lighting when possible.
