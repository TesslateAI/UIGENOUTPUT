Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FitFare Meal Prep

How do I customize my meals?

Our mobile app allows you to customize your meals by adjusting macros, adding or removing ingredients, and setting reminders. You can also request dietary modifications for any meal.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your meals, simply return them for a full refund. No questions asked.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes! We have special vegetarian and vegan plans available. You can select these when choosing your plan or customize your existing plan to exclude meat products.

How are the meals prepared?

Our meals are prepared in our state-of-the-art facilities using fresh ingredients. Each meal is portioned, labeled, and packaged to ensure safety and freshness. We follow strict hygiene protocols.
