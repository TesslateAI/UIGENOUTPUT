Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Questions

Find quick answers to your questions about online physiotherapy.

Online physiotherapy involves a virtual video consultation with a licensed physiotherapist. You'll discuss your injury, receive a diagnosis, and get a personalized recovery plan, including exercises and advice, via email or a secure platform.

For many common sports injuries, online physiotherapy has been shown to be just as effective as traditional in-person sessions, especially when combined with a strong home exercise program. It offers the added benefit of convenience and flexibility.

You'll need a stable internet connection, a device with a webcam and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone), and a quiet, private space to perform exercises. Comfortable clothing is also recommended.
