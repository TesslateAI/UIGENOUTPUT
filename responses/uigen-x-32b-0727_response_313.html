Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GreenCycle

What materials can I compost?

GreenCycle can handle most kitchen scraps including fruit and vegetable peels, coffee grounds, eggshells, tea bags, and small amounts of food-soiled paper. Avoid meat, dairy, and oily foods as they can attract pests and create odors.

How often do I need to add material?

You can add material daily or as you generate it. The unit processes waste continuously, but we recommend adding no more than 2-3 pounds per day to maintain optimal conditions. The smart sensors will notify you when the bin is full.

How do I maintain the unit?

Maintenance is minimal. You'll need to empty the compost drawer every 4-6 weeks and replace the carbon filter every 6 months. The unit is also dishwasher safe for easy cleaning. We provide a comprehensive maintenance guide with each unit.

What if I have a power outage?

GreenCycle has a 24-hour battery backup that keeps the system running during short outages. For extended outages, the unit will enter a low-power mode that maintains basic functions. We recommend unplugging the unit during severe storms as a precaution.
