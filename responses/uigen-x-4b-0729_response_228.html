Everything you need to know about GreenBoost

For most house plants, we recommend applying GreenBoost every 2-3 weeks during the growing season (spring to fall). For flowering plants, we recommend using it weekly to promote blooms. Always follow the instructions on the package based on your plant type and size.

Absolutely! GreenBoost is made with natural, non-toxic ingredients and is safe for pets and children. However, we recommend keeping it out of reach of small children and pets while you're applying it. Once applied and watered in, it's completely safe.

We recommend using GreenBoost as your primary fertilizer and avoiding other fertilizers to prevent nutrient burn. However, if you need to supplement, you can use a balanced, water-soluble fertilizer at half the recommended strength once a month. Always check the labels for compatibility.

GreenBoost is suitable for all house plants, including but not limited to: house plants, succulents, cacti, ferns, spider plants, philodendrons, ZZ plants, and flowering plants like African violets and orchids. It's not recommended for plants that are already showing signs of over-fertilization.
