Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a solar panel system cost?

Our systems start at $15,000 before incentives. After federal and state tax credits, many homeowners install solar for little to no money upfront. We offer financing options for all budgets.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in 1-3 days. Our team handles all aspects from permitting to final inspection, so you can enjoy your solar system quickly.

What maintenance is required?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. We include a 25-year maintenance plan with all installations. Simply rinse panels occasionally, and we handle annual performance monitoring.

Do I still need a utility company?

Yes, but you'll use significantly less grid power. Most systems are grid-tied, allowing you to draw power at night and sell excess energy back to your utility company.
