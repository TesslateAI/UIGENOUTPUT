The Cost of Human Error

Data breaches often start with a single click, a misplaced document, or a failed password. Our research shows that a significant percentage of breaches are preventable with proper employee awareness.

Phishing Attacks

Employees are often the first line of defense against phishing emails. A single click can lead to credential theft and ransomware.

Weak Passwords

Reusing passwords or using easily guessable ones can leave your systems vulnerable to brute-force attacks.

Social Engineering

Sophisticated social engineering tactics can manipulate employees into divulging sensitive information or performing unauthorized actions.
