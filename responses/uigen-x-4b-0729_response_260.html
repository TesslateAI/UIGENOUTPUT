Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our courses

Do I need prior experience to take these courses?

We offer courses for all levels. Our beginner courses start with fundamental concepts and gradually progress to more advanced techniques. Most courses require basic DIY skills like using a hammer and measuring, which are typically learned in elementary school.

How long do I have access to the courses?

For our monthly plans, you have lifetime access to all course materials. For our one-time purchase plans, you get access for 1 year. After that, you can still access course content, but you won't receive any new content added after your purchase.

Can I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes! All our courses include a certificate of completion that you can share on LinkedIn or with potential clients. Premium members also get access to a portfolio template to showcase your new skills.

What if I need help during my course?

Our Pro and Premium members have access to our expert Q&A forum where you can ask questions and get answers from instructors and other students. Premium members also get priority support and can schedule one-on-one consultations with instructors.
