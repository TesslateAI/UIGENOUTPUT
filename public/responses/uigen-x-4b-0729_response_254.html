Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our personalized vitamin subscriptions.

How do I get a personalized vitamin plan?

Simply complete our quick health assessment survey. We'll analyze your answers to create a customized plan tailored to your age, health goals, and current lifestyle. Our experts will then recommend the perfect combination of vitamins and supplements for your needs.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely! Your health journey is dynamic, and your needs may change over time. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Changes to your plan will take effect immediately for the next billing cycle.

What if I'm on medication?

We take this very seriously. Our personalized plans are designed to complement your medication regimen, not interfere with it. During your initial assessment, please inform us of any medications you're taking. Our team will ensure that our recommendations are safe and effective when used together.

How quickly do I receive my order?

We prioritize fast delivery for your convenience. Most orders are processed and shipped within 24 hours. You can choose between standard delivery (2-3 business days) or express shipping (next business day) at checkout. Once shipped, you'll receive tracking information.
