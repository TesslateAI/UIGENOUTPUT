Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CampSpark.

CampSpark offers programs for children aged 6 to 14, divided into age-appropriate groups to ensure a safe and engaging experience for everyone.

We maintain a low staff-to-camper ratio, typically 1:6 for younger campers and 1:8 for older campers, ensuring personalized attention and safety.

We provide a detailed packing list during registration. It typically includes clothing, toiletries, a reusable water bottle, and any specific items needed for special activities.

While we encourage campers to disconnect from technology, we offer scheduled "phone hours" or message boards for parents to send short notes. We prioritize a focused camp experience.
