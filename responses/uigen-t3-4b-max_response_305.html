Hands-on Learning, Future-ready Skills

Our robotics kits are designed to transform abstract concepts into tangible experiences. Children will physically build robots, then use intuitive coding to make them move, react, and solve problems. This approach fosters critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills in a fun and engaging way.

Boosts Cognitive Development

Improves logical reasoning, spatial awareness, and mathematical understanding through practical application.

What's Inside a CodeKids Kit?

The Classic Explorer Kit

Ideal for beginners, this kit teaches the basics of robotics and simple coding concepts. Children will build a basic robot that can move, turn, and respond to simple commands.

  • 5+ Servo Motors
  • 12+ LED Lights
  • Pre-loaded Coding Software
  • Easy-to-follow manual

The Advanced Engineer Kit

For aspiring engineers, this kit introduces more complex mechanics and advanced coding, enabling kids to build more sophisticated robots and control their movements with greater precision.

  • 10+ Servo Motors & Actuators

The Coding Innovator Kit

This kit is for the most advanced learners, focusing on complex coding logic, sensor integration, and robotics design. Children will tackle real-world challenges and create their own innovative robots.
