Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our gardening kits.

How long does it take to see results?
Most plants will start producing within 2-4 weeks. Herbs typically mature faster, while leafy greens might take a bit longer. With consistent care, you can harvest fresh produce within 3-6 months.
What if I don't have a sunny windowsill?
While sunlight is ideal, many plants can thrive in indirect light. We recommend using grow lights for low-light conditions. Our kits include instructions for using grow lights effectively.
Can I grow in a shady area?
Yes! Some plants like spinach, kale, and certain herbs thrive in shade. We have specific recommendations for low-light areas in our detailed planting guides.
What plants are included in the Starter Kit?
The Starter Kit includes 10 different seed packets for herbs and leafy greens. Specifically: basil, mint, parsley, cilantro, dill, arugula, spinach, kale, lettuce, and radishes. Instructions for optimal growth are included.
How often should I water the plants?
Most plants require watering once a week, but this can vary based on environmental conditions. Our kits include a watering schedule based on your location. Remember to water from the bottom to prevent leaf diseases.
