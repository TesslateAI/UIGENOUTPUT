Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HopeGivers and how you can help.

How is my donation used?

+

At least 96% of all donations go directly to program expenses, administrative costs are covered by the remaining 4%. We are committed to transparency and efficiency in how we use your money.

Can I make a recurring donation?

+

Yes! Monthly giving allows us to plan for the future and maximize our impact. Your consistent support helps us provide sustainable solutions to community challenges.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

+

Yes, HopeGivers is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes.

How can I get involved beyond donating?

+

We offer various volunteer opportunities, both locally and internationally. You can also help by spreading awareness on social media, joining our advocacy campaigns, or hosting fundraising events.
