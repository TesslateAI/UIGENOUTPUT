Our Story

Founded in 2010 by pastry chef Elena Rodriguez, Sweet Haven began as a small passion project in her kitchen. What started as weekend baking experiments has grown into a beloved community bakery.

We believe in the power of fresh, handmade ingredients and the joy of creating something delicious. Our philosophy is simple: bake with heart, using locally sourced ingredients and traditional techniques passed down through generations.
