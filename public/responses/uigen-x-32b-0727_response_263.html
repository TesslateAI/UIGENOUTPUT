Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does the organizing process take?

The time required depends on the size of your home and the scope of the project. A typical session takes 4-6 hours. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our free consultation.

Do you handle both residential and commercial organizing?

Yes! We provide organizing services for both homes and offices. Our team is experienced in creating efficient spaces for all types of environments.

What is your payment policy?

We require a 50% deposit to book your session, with the balance due on the day of service. We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cash.

Do you offer ongoing support after the initial session?

Yes! Our Complete Harmony package includes 3 months of support. We also offer maintenance sessions for all clients at a discounted rate.
