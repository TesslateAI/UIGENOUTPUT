Our Story

Founded in 2010, Heritage Treasures began as a passion project for our family. What started as a small collection in our garage has grown into a cherished business dedicated to preserving the craftsmanship of bygone eras.

We believe that antiques are more than just objects—they are stories, emotions, and connections to the past. Each piece we curate is carefully inspected, restored with traditional techniques, and authenticated by our team of expert appraisers.
