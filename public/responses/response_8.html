Who We Are

Grace Church is a welcoming and diverse community dedicated to following Jesus Christ. We believe in living out our faith through love, service, and a commitment to growing in our relationship with God and each other.

Our mission is to make disciples of Jesus Christ by sharing the Gospel, building a supportive fellowship, and engaging in compassionate outreach to our local community and beyond.
