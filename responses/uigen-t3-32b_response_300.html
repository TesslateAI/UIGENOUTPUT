THE STORY BEGINS...

A World on the Brink of Eclipse

In the fractured realm of Elyria, the Forgotten Throne lies dormant, its ancient magic sealed away by the last guardians. But when a mysterious eclipse begins to spread, consuming villages and twisting nature itself, the delicate balance of power is threatened.

Our protagonist, Elara, a skilled but disillusioned scholar, stumbles upon a cryptic prophecy hinting at her bloodline's connection to the throne. Reluctantly drawn into a perilous journey, she must confront ancient evils, forge uneasy alliances, and discover her true destiny to prevent the world from plunging into eternal darkness.
