Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the HomeSense Starter Kit?

The starter kit includes 2 Smart Door/Window Sensors, 1 Smart Indoor Camera, 1 Smart Hub, and optional 1 Smart Door Lock. The hub is essential for all devices to connect.

How easy is it to install?

Our devices are designed for easy, DIY installation. Most require no drilling, just adhesive or simple mounting. The setup process is guided by our user-friendly app.

What kind of alerts do I get?

You'll receive instant notifications on your phone for motion detection, door/window openings, and any unusual activity, allowing you to respond quickly.

Is my data secure?

Yes, HomeSense prioritizes your privacy. We use end-to-end encryption for all data transmissions and store video footage securely in the cloud.
