Our Story

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiasts Maria and James, Bean & Brew began as a small caf√© with a big dream. We believed that coffee should be more than just a morning ritual - it should be an experience.

Our journey took us to coffee farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala where we formed direct relationships with growers committed to sustainable practices. We source only the finest beans, roasted in-house to perfection.

Today, Bean & Brew has become a community hub where locals gather to enjoy exceptional coffee, delicious pastries, and the warm atmosphere we're known for.
