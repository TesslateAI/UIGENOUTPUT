Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pet sitting services

We care for all types of pets including dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, hamsters, and small mammals. We have experience with both common and exotic pets.

Simply fill out our online quote form with your pet's details and your availability. We'll get back to you within 24 hours with a personalized quote.

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may incur a fee of 50% of the service cost.

We are not equipped to provide medical care. In case of an emergency, we will contact you immediately and help you get to a vet. We carry basic first-aid supplies for minor injuries.
