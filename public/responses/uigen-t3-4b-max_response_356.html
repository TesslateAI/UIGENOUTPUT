Our Comprehensive Course Modules

Our piano learning journey is broken down into structured modules, designed to build your skills progressively.

1. Introduction to the Piano

Learn the basics of the piano, including its structure, reading sheet music, and understanding notes.

2. Basic Scales and Chords

Master major and minor scales, and build fundamental chord progressions for your playing.

3. Playing Simple Melodies

Start composing and playing your own simple melodies, building confidence and creativity.

4. Reading and Rhythm

Improve your sight-reading and rhythmic accuracy for all levels of music.

5. Repertoire & Performance

Learn popular piano pieces and develop your stage presence and performance skills.

6. Advanced Techniques

Explore more complex techniques like finger exercises, legato, and advanced pedaling.
