What is Run for a Cause?

Our annual Charity Run is a vibrant event designed to bring together individuals, families, and teams for a day of fitness, fun, and philanthropy.

Your Participation, Their Future

Every registration and donation directly contributes to supporting local charities that focus on education, health, and community development. It's more than just a run; it's a powerful way to make an impact.

  • 5K and 10K race distances
  • Fun activities for all ages
  • Medals and refreshments for all finishers
  • Local vendors and entertainment
