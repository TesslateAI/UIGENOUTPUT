Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GreenCycle compost bins.

How much space do I need for a GreenCycle bin?

Our standard GreenCycle bin requires a minimum space of 12" x 12" x 16". It's designed to fit in small spaces like balconies, patios, or even a corner of a small apartment. We also offer compact models for those with limited space.

What materials can I compost in GreenCycle?

GreenCycle accepts all organic kitchen waste including fruits and vegetables, coffee grounds, tea leaves, eggshells, and paper. We recommend avoiding meat, dairy, and oily foods as they can attract pests. We also recommend using our starter kit to introduce beneficial microbes.

How long does it take to turn waste into compost?

Under ideal conditions, GreenCycle can produce usable compost in 2-3 months. The exact time depends on factors like the amount of material, how often you stir and water, and the climate. We provide weekly reminders to maintain optimal conditions.

Is the GreenCycle bin odor-free?

Yes! Our advanced aeration system and odor-control technology ensure your compost bin remains odor-free. The design prevents gases from escaping while allowing for proper ventilation. We've been independently tested and certified as meeting the highest odor control standards.

What if I forget to stir or water my bin?

Our smart system includes sensors that monitor moisture and activity. If conditions become too dry or stagnant, the app will notify you. We also recommend stirring at least once a week to maintain proper aeration. The bin has a built-in timer that sends reminders to check on your compost.
