Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom pet portrait service

A typical portrait session lasts about 30-45 minutes. We work with your pet at their own pace, ensuring they feel comfortable. If your pet needs more time, we can schedule a second session at no additional cost.

We have a calm, safe environment with treats and toys to help your pet feel at ease. If your pet becomes too nervous, we'll pause and come back another time. We prioritize your pet's comfort above all else.

We offer several portrait styles to capture your pet's personality: 1) Natural Elegance - focusing on posture and grace, 2) Playful Energy - capturing movement and activity, 3) Expressive Features - highlighting eyes, ears, and personality, and 4) Lifestyle - showing your pet in action.

After your session, you'll receive a digital preview within 2-3 business days. Once you approve, we begin the final artwork, which typically takes 2-4 weeks to complete.
