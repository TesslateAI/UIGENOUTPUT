Crafting Time Since the Roaring Twenties

Chronomaster was founded in 1923 by master watchmaker Émile Rossi, who sought to create timepieces that combined Swiss precision with artistic elegance. Our heritage is built on a foundation of innovation and craftsmanship that spans nearly a century.

Each generation of watchmakers has added to our legacy, perfecting techniques and pushing boundaries while maintaining the timeless aesthetic that defines our brand.
