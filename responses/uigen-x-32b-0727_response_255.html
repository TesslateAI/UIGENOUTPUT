Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the ProGrow Mentorship program.

Our program pairs you with a mentor based on your career goals and industry. You'll have regular video calls, access to resources, and opportunities to work on real projects. The program is structured to provide consistent guidance over 6-12 months.

We provide personalized career strategy sessions, resume and LinkedIn optimization, interview preparation, skill development resources, and networking opportunities. Mentors also provide ongoing feedback and guidance throughout your career journey.

We use a comprehensive matching process based on your career goals, industry, experience level, and personal preferences. Our algorithm considers mentor expertise, availability, and compatibility to ensure the best possible match.

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not fully satisfied with your mentor match within the first month, we'll find you a new mentor at no additional cost. Our goal is to ensure you have the best possible experience.

We offer flexible pricing based on your needs and budget. Our standard plan starts at $499/month, which includes 4 hours of mentorship per month. We also offer discounted packages for longer commitments and group enrollments.
