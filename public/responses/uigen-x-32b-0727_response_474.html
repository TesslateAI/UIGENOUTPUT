Our Story

Founded in 2005 by brothers Marcus and Ethan Stone, Stone Heart Brewery began in a small garage with just two homebrew kits and a dream. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a community institution.

Our philosophy is simple: use the finest ingredients, respect traditional brewing methods, and create beers that tell a story. Each batch is crafted with care and attention to detail, ensuring a quality experience with every pint.

Today, we're proud to be a cornerstone of the Portland craft beer scene, serving our community with passion and purpose.

Marcus Stone

Founder & Head Brewer
