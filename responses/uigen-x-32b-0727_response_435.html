Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Nexus Watch

The Nexus Watch offers up to 72 hours of battery life on a single charge with typical use. With heavy usage including constant GPS tracking and frequent notifications, you can expect around 48 hours. The watch charges fully in just 15 minutes for a full day of use.

Yes, the Nexus Watch is fully compatible with both iOS and Android devices. It connects seamlessly with smartphones via Bluetooth and offers the same feature set across both platforms. The companion app is available on both App Store and Google Play.

The Nexus Watch features premium materials including aerospace-grade titanium for the case, sapphire crystal for the display, and medical-grade silicone for the band. The Ultimate edition features a platinum case for an even more luxurious feel.

Yes, all models are water resistant up to 50 meters (Classic) or 100 meters (Ultimate). You can swim, shower, and sweat with confidence. The watch also includes swim tracking features that automatically detect and record your swimming sessions.
