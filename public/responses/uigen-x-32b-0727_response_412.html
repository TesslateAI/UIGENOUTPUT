Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our VR experience

Do I need prior VR experience?

No prior experience is required! Our staff will guide you through the setup and controls before you start your session. We provide tutorials for first-time users.

What should I wear for my session?

Comfortable clothing is recommended. We provide VR headsets and motion controllers, but you'll want to wear shoes with non-slip soles. Avoid loose clothing that might get caught on equipment.

Is there an age limit for VR sessions?

Our standard sessions are for ages 12 and up. We offer special family packages for younger players (8+ with parental supervision). Children under 8 are not recommended for VR experiences.

Can I bring my own VR headset?

While we provide state-of-the-art VR equipment, you're welcome to bring your own compatible headset for custom sessions. Please contact us in advance to arrange this.
