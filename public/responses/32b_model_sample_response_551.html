Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

What is the age requirement for daycare?

Dogs must be at least 12 weeks old, fully vaccinated, and spayed/neutered (if applicable) to join our daycare program.

How do I enroll my dog?

You can start by scheduling a tour. During the tour, we'll discuss your dog's needs, answer any questions, and provide all the necessary information to begin the enrollment process.

What kind of activities do you offer?

We offer a variety of supervised play sessions, interactive games, obstacle courses, and sensory enrichment activities tailored to different age groups and energy levels.

What if my dog has special needs?

We welcome dogs with special needs! Please discuss your dog's specific requirements during the tour so we can ensure we can provide the best care.
