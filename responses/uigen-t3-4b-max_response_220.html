What age group is Crafty Kids Club for?

Each box includes all the materials, clear instructions, and a fun, engaging craft project, often with an educational theme.

What if my child is too advanced or too young for a box?

We offer a variety of themes and project difficulties. If you have specific needs, please contact us!
