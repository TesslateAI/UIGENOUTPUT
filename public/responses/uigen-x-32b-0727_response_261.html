FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our branding services.

How long does a brand audit take?

Our comprehensive brand audit typically takes 3-5 business days to complete. After submitting your information, we'll schedule a call to discuss our findings and recommendations.

What's included in the brand audit?

Our brand audit includes an analysis of your current branding, market positioning, visual identity, messaging, and customer perception. We provide actionable recommendations to improve your brand performance.

How much does your branding service cost?

We offer several tiered packages to suit different business needs and budgets. Our most popular package starts at $2,499 and includes a complete brand identity package. We also offer a free brand audit to help you determine the best approach.

Can you help with rebranding?

Absolutely! We specialize in rebranding for businesses looking to refresh their image, enter new markets, or better align with their evolving values. Our process ensures a smooth transition with minimal disruption to your business.
