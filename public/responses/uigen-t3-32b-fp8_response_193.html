Our Advanced Filtration Technology

PureFlow Water Filters utilize a multi-stage filtration system designed to remove a wide range of contaminants, ensuring water that's truly pure and great-tasting.

  • Stage 1: Sediment Filter - Removes large particles like sand, silt, and rust.
  • Stage 2: Activated Carbon Block - Eliminates chlorine, bad odors, and volatile organic compounds (VOCs).
  • Stage 3: Reverse Osmosis Membrane - Filters out dissolved solids, heavy metals, and other microscopic impurities.
  • Stage 4: Post-Activated Carbon Filter - Polishes the water, removing any remaining taste or odor.
