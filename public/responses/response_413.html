Our Story

Ceramica Handmade was founded on the belief that every piece of pottery holds a story, a touch, and a soul. We are a small, family-run studio dedicated to the ancient art of hand-thrown and hand-molded ceramics.

Our journey began with a passion for clay and a desire to create objects of beauty and utility that connect with people on a deeper level. Each piece is meticulously crafted, glazed, and fired, ensuring both aesthetic appeal and lasting quality.

The Art of Creation

Discover the meticulous steps involved in bringing a piece of Ceramica Handmade to life.

Preparing the Clay

We start with high-quality, locally sourced clay, carefully wedging it to remove air bubbles.

Hand-Throwing

Each piece is meticulously shaped on the wheel, a process requiring immense skill and patience.

Glazing

We apply a range of hand-mixed glazes, each contributing to the unique character of the piece.

Firing

The final stage involves high-temperature kiln firing, transforming raw clay into durable, beautiful ceramics.
