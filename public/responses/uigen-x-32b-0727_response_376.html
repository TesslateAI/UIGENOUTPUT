Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about personalized vitamin subscriptions

How does the genetic testing work?

We provide a simple saliva collection kit. Once we receive your sample, our lab analyzes specific genetic markers related to nutrient metabolism. This information helps us create a truly personalized vitamin regimen tailored to how your body processes nutrients.

Can I adjust my subscription later?

Absolutely! Your health needs can change, and so can your vitamin regimen. You can update your preferences, health goals, or even pause your subscription at any time through your online dashboard. We'll adjust your formula accordingly.

Are your vitamins organic and non-GMO?

Yes, we're committed to quality. All our vitamins are made with organic, non-GMO ingredients whenever possible. We source from ethical suppliers and ensure our products meet the highest standards for purity and effectiveness.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

We accommodate various dietary needs including vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free. During your initial assessment, you'll specify any restrictions or allergies, and our system will create a formula that works for you.

How soon will I see results?

Many customers report feeling more energetic within the first month. However, for optimal results, we recommend following your personalized regimen for at least 3 months. Nutrient levels build up gradually, and individual results may vary based on starting health status.
