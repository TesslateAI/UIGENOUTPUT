Ethical Sourcing at Our Core

Every piece in our collection is crafted with sustainability in mind. We partner with farms and factories that prioritize:

Organic Materials

Cotton, hemp, and linen grown without harmful pesticides or synthetic fertilizers.

Fair Trade Practices

Ensuring fair wages and safe working conditions for all workers in our supply chain.

Circular Economy

Designing products for longevity and offering recycling programs for end-of-life garments.

Our Sustainable Materials

We carefully select each material to ensure minimal environmental impact and maximum quality.

Organic Cotton

Grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, using 91% less water than conventional cotton.

Hemp Fabric

Requires no pesticides, grows quickly, and uses 50% less water than cotton. Naturally resistant to insects.

Recycled Wool

Sourced from post-consumer sweaters and garments, reducing landfill waste and extending fiber life.

Tencel™ Lyocell

Made from sustainably harvested wood pulp, processed in a closed-loop system that recycles 99% of solvents.
