Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HomeSense security.

How long does it take to install HomeSense?

Installation typically takes 15-20 minutes. Most users can set up their entire system in under an hour with our detailed instructions.

Do I need a professional installer?

No, HomeSense is designed for DIY installation. We provide all necessary hardware and tools in your starter kit.

What happens if I forget to arm my system?

If your system is disarmed when you leave, it will automatically arm itself when you leave. You can also set up scheduled arming.

How long does the battery last?

Our sensors use 10-year batteries. With full power consumption, you'd need replacements every 10 years. But our system uses 12-month battery life for cameras.

Can I monitor my home remotely?

Yes! With our mobile app, you can monitor your home from anywhere. You can view live cameras, receive alerts, and control your system.
