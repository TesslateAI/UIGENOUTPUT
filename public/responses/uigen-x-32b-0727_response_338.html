Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PocketFinance

Is PocketFinance free to use?

Yes! PocketFinance is completely free for all students. We offer premium features in the future, but the core app will always be free.

How does the budget tracking work?

You can set monthly budgets for different categories like food, entertainment, and transportation. The app will track your spending and send notifications when you're approaching your limits.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Absolutely! Your data syncs automatically between all your devices when you sign in with your account.

How secure is my financial data?

We take security seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties.

Do I need to enter every expense manually?

No! You can scan receipts with our camera feature, and we'll automatically extract the information. You can also set up recurring expenses to save time.
