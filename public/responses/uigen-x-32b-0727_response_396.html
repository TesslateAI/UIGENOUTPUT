Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our craft kits

How often do I receive a craft kit?

With our monthly subscription, you'll receive one craft kit every month. The annual subscription includes 12 kits delivered monthly over the course of a year.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Yes! You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time through your account. There are no cancellation fees.

Are the kits suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our kits are designed for all skill levels, with detailed instructions that make it easy for beginners to create beautiful crafts.

What if I'm not satisfied with a kit?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your kit, you can return it for a full refund.

Can I gift a subscription to someone else?

Yes! Our gift subscriptions make perfect presents. You can choose the duration and we'll send the kits directly to the recipient.
