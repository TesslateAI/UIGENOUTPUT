About GreenStreet Urban Farms

We are revolutionizing urban agriculture with our cutting-edge vertical farming technology. By growing produce indoors, year-round, we eliminate the need for long-distance transportation, ensuring unparalleled freshness and reducing our carbon footprint.

Our mission is to provide local restaurants with a consistent, high-quality supply of fresh, organic, and hyper-local produce, right at their doorstep. We believe in feeding communities sustainably, one plate at a time.

Committed to a Greener Future

GreenStreet Urban Farms is dedicated to sustainable practices that benefit both your kitchen and the planet.

  • Year-round local production, no long-haul transport.
  • 90% less water usage compared to traditional farming.
  • No pesticides or chemical fertilizers, 100% organic.
  • Minimal waste and a closed-loop system.
