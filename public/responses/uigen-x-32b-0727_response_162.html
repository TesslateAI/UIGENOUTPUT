About Generative Art

Generative art is a form of digital art created using algorithms and computational processes. Our interface uses complex mathematical patterns combined with random elements to create unique visual experiences.

Each piece is procedurally generated, meaning no two artworks are exactly the same. The interactive elements allow you to influence the creative process in real-time.
