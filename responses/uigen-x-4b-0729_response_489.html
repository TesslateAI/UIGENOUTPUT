Success Stories

Clean Water in Kenya

In the remote village of Maua, 85% of children suffered from waterborne diseases. With your help, we installed a solar-powered water purification system that now provides clean water to 500 families.

Kenya

Education in Bangladesh

Bangladesh

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work and how you can help.

How will my donation be used?

95% of your donation goes directly to program expenses, with 5% covering administrative costs and 1% supporting fundraising efforts. We publish detailed reports on our annual impact report.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, HopeBridge is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for tax purposes.

How can I get involved beyond donating?

Join our volunteer program, participate in our annual fundraising events, become a monthly donor, or help spread awareness on social media. Visit our "Get Involved" page for more options.

How often do you publish financial reports?

We are committed to transparency. We publish our annual financial report and impact statement online each year. Additionally, we provide monthly updates on our website and quarterly reports to donors.
