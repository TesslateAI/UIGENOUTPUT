Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about solar energy

What is the average savings with solar panels?

Homeowners typically save between 50-80% on their electricity bills after installing solar panels. The exact savings depend on your energy consumption, solar output, and local utility rates.

How long does the average solar panel system last?

Most solar panel systems come with a 25-year performance warranty. With proper maintenance, they can produce 80-90% of their initial capacity after 25 years. Many systems are still producing significant energy after 30 years.

Can I get a loan for solar panel installation?

Yes! Many financial institutions offer specialized solar loans. SolarSpark partners with lenders to provide flexible financing options with competitive rates. You can also qualify for government incentives like the Federal Solar Tax Credit.

How quickly can I get my solar panels installed?

Most solar installations are completed within 2-4 weeks after the initial assessment. Our streamlined process includes scheduling, permitting, installation, and system activation. We work to minimize your downtime.
