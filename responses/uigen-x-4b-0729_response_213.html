Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our corporate wellness programs

How long does it take to see results?

Most companies see measurable improvements within 3-6 months of implementing a wellness program. We measure success through employee surveys, productivity metrics, and health data.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer tiered pricing based on company size and specific program needs. Our Health & Wellness program starts at $1,200/year per employee, while our comprehensive Mindfulness program begins at $1,500/year per employee. We offer custom packages for larger organizations.

Do you offer custom programs?

Yes, we specialize in custom corporate wellness programs tailored to your specific business needs, employee demographics, and budget. Our team will work with you to design the perfect solution.

How do you measure program effectiveness?

We use a combination of quantitative metrics (productivity, absenteeism, healthcare costs) and qualitative feedback (employee surveys, focus groups). All data is reported monthly to you.
