Bespoke Services

From traditional tailoring to contemporary designs, we offer a full range of personalized services.

Full Bespoke

Our signature service involves multiple fittings and complete customization. From fabric selection to final adjustments, we craft a garment that is uniquely yours.

  • 3-4 fittings
  • Complete customization
  • Premium fabric selection
  • Hand-stitched details

Made-to-Measure

A streamlined approach offering personalized service with fewer fittings. Perfect for those seeking a tailored look with efficiency.

  • 2 fittings
  • Custom sizing
  • Limited customization options
  • Premium fabric selection
