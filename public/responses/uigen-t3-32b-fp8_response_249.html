How Our Membership Works

Joining our community garden is simple and rewarding. Follow these easy steps to start your green journey.

1. Choose Your Plot

Select a sun-drenched plot or join a shared community bed. We have options for all experience levels.

2. Join the Community

Meet fellow gardeners, attend workshops, and participate in garden events.

3. Harvest & Enjoy

Enjoy the fruits (and vegetables!) of your labor, fresh from your own garden.
