Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of sports injuries do you treat?

We specialize in a wide range of sports injuries, including sprains, strains, knee, shoulder, and ankle injuries, as well as common overuse injuries like tendonitis.

Is my data secure during online sessions?

Yes, all our online sessions are conducted via encrypted video calls, ensuring your privacy and data security. We use industry-standard protocols to protect your information.

What is the cost of a virtual physio session?

Pricing for our virtual physio sessions is transparent and competitive. You can find our current rates on the "Pricing" page, or contact us for a personalized quote.

Do I need to download any specific software?

No, you don't need to download any additional software. Our platform is designed to work with your existing devices and internet connection, making it easy to start your session.
