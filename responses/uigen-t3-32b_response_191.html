Frequently Asked Questions

Are Heritage Hues dyes truly natural?

Yes, all our dyes are derived from 100% natural plant-based sources, free from synthetic chemicals.

What kind of fabrics can I dye with these kits?

Our kits are designed for natural fibers like cotton, linen, silk, and wool for best results.

Do I need any special equipment?

Most kits include essential items, but you'll need basic kitchen tools like a large pot and a stirring spoon.

How long do the dyes last?

Stored properly, the dyes in our kits have a shelf life of 1-2 years.
