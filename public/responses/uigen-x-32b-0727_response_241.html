Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the program

Do I need any prior coding experience?

No prior coding experience is required. Our program is designed for complete beginners. The free introductory course is perfect for exploring coding before committing to the full program.

How long does it take to complete the program?

The full bootcamp is a 6-month intensive program. Most students complete it in that time, but you have up to 12 months to finish. The free introductory course is just 5 days.

What if I don't find a job after graduation?

We offer a 100% job guarantee. If you complete the program and don't find a job within 6 months, we'll refund your tuition and continue to provide job search support until you're employed.

Can I work while attending the bootcamp?

Many students work part-time while attending. Our program is designed to be intensive but flexible. We recommend dedicating at least 30 hours per week to the program for optimal results.
