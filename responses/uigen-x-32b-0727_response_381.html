About the Author

Eleanor Vance

Eleanor Vance is the award-winning author of the bestselling fantasy series "The Chronicles of the Forgotten." With a background in ancient languages and mythology, she brings a unique depth to her world-building.

When not writing, Eleanor lectures on comparative mythology at the University of Eldoria and explores ancient ruins in search of inspiration.
