Master the Gameplay

Eldoria: The Forgotten Kingdom offers a unique blend of strategy and adventure. Here's a quick overview of the core mechanics.

Core Loop

Players take turns performing actions, including moving heroes, gathering resources, discovering relics, and engaging in combat.

  • Action Points: Spend AP to move and perform actions.
  • Resource Management: Collect and spend resources to upgrade heroes and build structures.
  • Combat System: Engage in tactical combat using hero abilities and magic.

Magic & Abilities

Each hero possesses unique abilities and can learn powerful spells from the ancient archives.

  • Hero Abilities: Special powers unique to each hero.
  • Spells: Cast powerful spells from the spellbook, with varying costs and effects.
  • Relic Powers: Uncover ancient relics to gain permanent bonuses and unique effects.
