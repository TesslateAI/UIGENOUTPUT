Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and services

How does the virtual tour work?

Our virtual tours use advanced 360° camera technology to create immersive property tours. After signing up, you can access these tours directly from our platform. Simply click the "Virtual Tour" button on any property listing to experience it.

Do I need to be a real estate agent to use your service?

No, our platform is designed for both buyers and sellers. Homeowners can list their properties, while buyers can search and explore listings. We also offer services for real estate agents to enhance their listings.

What makes your virtual tours different from others?

Our virtual tours feature 4K resolution, smooth navigation, and interactive elements. We also provide downloadable floor plans and architectural details. Our proprietary technology ensures a seamless experience on all devices.

How long does it take to list a property?

Most properties are listed within 1-3 business days. The process involves uploading property details, professional photography, and our virtual tour creation. We work efficiently to get your property on the market quickly.
