Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the PawPrint tracker

Attaching the PawPrint tracker is simple! Just clip it onto your pet's collar using the secure clip mechanism. The tracker is designed to be comfortable and won't irritate your pet. For small pets, we also offer a special attachment method using a harness or a bandana.

The PawPrint GPS tracker has a range of up to 10 miles from your phone. This range can be extended with our optional satellite tracker for remote areas. The device uses both GPS and Wi-Fi to provide accurate location tracking even in urban environments.

PawPrint uses high-precision GPS technology with an accuracy of within 50 feet. The device updates your pet's location every 30 seconds, providing real-time tracking. For maximum accuracy, we recommend using the device in areas with clear satellite reception.

The PawPrint tracker has a gentle vibration and a small LED light to encourage your pet to wear it. If your pet is resistant, we recommend starting with short periods and gradually increasing wear time. For severe resistance, we offer a special "training collar" that helps with acclimation.
