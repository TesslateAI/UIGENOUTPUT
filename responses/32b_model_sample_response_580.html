Is Your Pet Showing These Common Behavioral Issues?

You're not alone. Many pet owners face challenges that can feel overwhelming. PetWellness Coach is here to help.

Excessive Barking

Constant barking that disrupts your peace and potentially causes neighbor complaints.

Aggression or Fear

Unpredictable reactions to people, other pets, or new situations that can be distressing.

Separation Anxiety

Destructive behavior or excessive vocalization when you're away, causing guilt and stress.

Resource Guarding

Protectiveness over food, toys, or people, leading to potential conflict.

Leash Reactivity

Pulling, lunging, or barking on walks, making outings stressful and unpleasant.

House Training Issues

Accidents indoors despite training, causing frustration and hygiene concerns.
