About the Exhibition

"Ancient Wonders: Treasures of Mesopotamia" presents a rare opportunity to witness over 200 exquisite artifacts, including cuneiform tablets, intricate pottery, and exquisite jewelry, spanning thousands of years of history.

Key Highlights:

  • Discover the origins of writing with ancient cuneiform tablets.
  • Explore the artistic mastery of Sumerian and Babylonian pottery.
  • Admire the intricate gold and lapis lazuli jewelry from the Royal Tombs of Ur.
  • Interactive displays and guided tours available for a deeper understanding.
