Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our retirement planning services

How much does your financial planning service cost?

We offer a free 30-minute initial consultation to discuss your needs. After that, our planning services are fee-only, with transparent pricing based on the scope of services. There are no hidden fees or commissions.

Do you manage investments as well?

Yes, we offer comprehensive investment management services. After developing your retirement strategy, we can help implement and manage your investment portfolio to help you achieve your financial goals.

How soon should I start retirement planning?

The earlier you start, the better. However, it's never too late to begin planning. Our approach is designed to help you at any stage of your retirement journey, whether you're 5-10 years away or considering retirement immediately.

What makes your approach different?

We focus exclusively on retirement planning for pre-retirees, allowing us to develop deep expertise in this specialized area. Our fee-only model ensures we act in your best interest without conflicts of interest. We also prioritize comprehensive, holistic planning that considers all aspects of your financial life.
