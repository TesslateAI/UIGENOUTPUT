Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our home security system

How does the 24/7 monitoring work?

Our certified monitoring team is on duty 24/7, 365 days a year. When our system detects an alarm, our team verifies the situation through live camera feeds and contacts you immediately. If we don't receive a response, we dispatch emergency services as needed.

What happens during a power outage?

Our security system includes a backup battery that keeps all devices powered for up to 24 hours during an outage. The monitoring center will also be notified of any power interruptions and will monitor your property extra closely during this time.

Can I view my cameras from my phone?

Yes! Our mobile app allows you to view live feeds from all your cameras, receive instant alerts, and control your security system from anywhere. The app is available for both iOS and Android devices.

How long does installation take?

Installation typically takes 2-4 hours for most homes, depending on the size and complexity. Our technicians will complete the entire setup, including camera placement, sensor installation, and system configuration.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service, you can cancel within the first month for a full refund. After that, we require 30 days notice for cancellation.
