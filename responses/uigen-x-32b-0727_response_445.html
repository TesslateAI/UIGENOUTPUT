About Nova Echo

I'm an electronic music producer and sound designer based in Berlin. My music blends retro synthwave aesthetics with modern experimental techniques, creating sonic landscapes that transport listeners to neon-lit futures and forgotten pasts.

With a background in audio engineering and a passion for analog synthesizers, I craft music that's both nostalgic and forward-looking. My work has been featured on international radio stations and streaming platforms, reaching listeners in over 50 countries.
