Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Data Analytics Course.

Do I need any prior experience with data?

No prior experience is required! Our course is designed specifically for beginners, starting from the very basics and building up your skills step-by-step.

What tools will I learn?

You'll gain hands-on experience with Excel, SQL, and Python, which are fundamental tools in the data analytics field. We focus on practical application from the start.

How long does the course take to complete?

The course is structured over 6 weeks, with recommended weekly study hours. You can complete it at your own pace, but we encourage consistent progress for the best learning experience.

Is there a capstone project?

Yes! The final module includes a capstone project where you'll apply all the skills you've learned to a real-world dataset, preparing you for practical challenges.
