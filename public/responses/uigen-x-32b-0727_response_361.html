Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BookLover Box

How does the book selection process work?

Our expert curators select books based on a combination of literary merit, diversity of content, and reader preferences. Each month, we consider new releases, classic titles, and hidden gems across various genres to create a well-rounded selection.

Can I choose my own books?

Yes! While we provide carefully curated selections, BookLover Box members can customize their monthly picks. Our platform allows you to rate books you've received, and our algorithm will use your preferences to suggest books that match your tastes.

What if I don't like a book I receive?

No problem! We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you don't love a book, you can return it for a full refund. We'll credit your account and use that information to improve future selections.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can pause your subscription for up to 3 months or cancel it at any time with no cancellation fees. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle.
