Deep Dives & Engaging Discussions

Our discussions go beyond surface-level opinions. We delve into themes, characters, and the emotional impact of feel-good fiction.

What We Discuss:

  • Themes & Symbolism: Uncover hidden meanings and explore the deeper messages within our chosen books.

  • Character Development: Analyze motivations, growth, and relationships of beloved characters.

  • Personal Connections: Share how stories resonate with our own lives and experiences.

  • Author's Perspective: Discuss the author's intent and creative choices behind the narrative.
