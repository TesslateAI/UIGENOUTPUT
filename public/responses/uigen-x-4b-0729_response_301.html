Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about solar panel installation.

On average, residential solar panel systems range from $5,000 to $20,000 depending on your energy needs and system size. However, many homeowners qualify for tax credits that can reduce the net cost significantly.

Most residential solar installations take between 1 to 3 days depending on system size and weather conditions. The actual installation process is relatively quick because it involves mounting panels and connecting them to your home's electrical system.

While solar panels require minimal maintenance, they do need occasional cleaning to remove dust and debris. Most modern systems come with a 20-25 year warranty that covers defects in materials and workmanship. Regular cleaning isn't necessary if your panels are in a clean environment.
