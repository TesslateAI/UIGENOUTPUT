Who We Serve

FutureFounders is designed for early-stage tech startups that have a validated product concept and are ready to scale. We invest in products that solve real problems with innovative technology.

Our program is open to founders from all backgrounds, with a focus on diversity and inclusion in the startup ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the FutureFounders Accelerator.

The FutureFounders Accelerator program runs for 12 weeks, with an additional 4-week demo period for final product refinement and investor pitches.

We provide access to a network of investors looking for promising early-stage tech companies. While we don't provide direct funding, we connect you with investors who may offer funding in the range of $50,000 to $500,000 depending on your stage and product.

Our program includes comprehensive mentorship, product development support, access to our network of investors and potential customers, and co-working space. We also provide training in business development, marketing, and fundraising.

While we prefer startups that have received some seed funding, we are open to applicants with pre-seed funding. Our program is designed for early-stage companies looking to accelerate their growth.
