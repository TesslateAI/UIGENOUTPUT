Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AssistPro

How long does it take to set up AssistPro?

Setting up AssistPro is incredibly simple. Most solopreneurs can get started in less than 15 minutes. Our guided onboarding process walks you through connecting your tools and configuring your preferences.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer multiple support channels including email, live chat, and a comprehensive knowledge base. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support with faster response times. We also have a community forum where you can ask questions and share experiences.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You'll continue to have access to your account until the end of your current billing period.

How secure is my data with AssistPro?

Security is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure cloud platforms with regular security audits. We also offer two-factor authentication for added security.
