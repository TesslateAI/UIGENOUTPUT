Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mobile grooming service

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment directly through our website using the form above. We also accept bookings via our mobile app and by calling our office. For medical grooming requests, please call us at least 48 hours in advance.

Is your service pet-friendly?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating a stress-free experience for pets. Our technicians are trained in gentle handling, positive reinforcement, and calming techniques to ensure your furry friend feels safe and comfortable.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire metropolitan area including all surrounding suburbs. For remote locations, please call us to see if we can accommodate your request. We'll make special arrangements when possible.

What if my pet is nervous during grooming?

We have multiple calming techniques to help nervous pets. Our technicians can use gentle music, toys, and positive reinforcement to create a relaxing atmosphere. If your pet is extremely nervous, we can schedule a session in our quietest location.
