The Cosmic Journey

In the year 2187, humanity has expanded beyond the solar system, but the mysteries of the cosmos remain. You play as an explorer in the Nexus Program, a joint initiative between Earth's leading space agencies and the newly formed Interstellar Alliance.

When a signal is detected from a distant galaxy - a signal that shouldn't exist - you're tasked with investigating. What begins as a routine exploration mission quickly becomes a journey through realities, challenging your understanding of space, time, and existence itself.
