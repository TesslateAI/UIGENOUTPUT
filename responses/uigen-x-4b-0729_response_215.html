Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coaching services

Most clients report significant changes within 2-4 sessions. The pace depends on individual commitment and the specific coaching goals. Many entrepreneurs see measurable improvements in mindset and behavior within the first month.

We offer a full refund within 30 days of your first session if you're not satisfied. Our goal is to ensure you feel comfortable and see value from the outset. We'll work together to determine if coaching is right for you.

Sessions can be rescheduled or cancelled up to 24 hours before your appointment without any charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a small administrative fee.

Success is measured through behavioral changes, mindset shifts, and measurable business improvements. We track progress through session feedback, action plan implementation, and periodic assessments. The goal is sustainable personal and professional growth.
