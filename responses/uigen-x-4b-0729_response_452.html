Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your journey

How does the map integration work?

Our map integration uses Leaflet.js to create an interactive map. Simply click on the map to add destinations, connect points with routes, and optimize your journey. All your itinerary details appear directly on the map.

Can I collaborate with travel companions?

Absolutely! Our platform supports real-time collaboration. Multiple users can edit the same itinerary simultaneously. Changes are saved automatically and everyone gets notified of updates.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, Voyage Planner is available on both iOS and Android apps. Download them from the App Store or Google Play. Your itinerary syncs automatically across all devices.

How do I share my itinerary with others?

Share your itinerary via email, direct link, or social media. Each itinerary has a unique shareable link that includes a password for privacy. Recipients can view, edit, or add to your plan.
