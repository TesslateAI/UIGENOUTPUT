About Carlos Ruiz

Carlos Ruiz is a contemporary electronic musician and producer, known for his innovative blend of ambient textures, pulsating rhythms, and emotive soundscapes. Hailing from the vibrant city of New York, Carlos has spent years crafting music that transcends genres, creating immersive experiences for listeners worldwide.

His journey began in the underground music scene, where he quickly gained recognition for his unique sound. With a keen ear for detail and a passion for storytelling through sound, Carlos continues to push the boundaries of electronic music, drawing inspiration from diverse cultures and experiences.

Beyond the studio, Carlos is an advocate for music education and community engagement, often collaborating with local artists and hosting workshops to nurture the next generation of musicians.
