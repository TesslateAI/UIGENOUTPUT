Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What types of training programs do you offer?

I offer a wide range of training programs including strength training, cardio conditioning, HIIT workouts, yoga, weight management, and rehabilitation programs. All programs are customized to meet individual goals and fitness levels.

What is your training session like?

Each session is personalized to your goals and includes warm-ups, skill drills, resistance training, and cool-down exercises. We focus on proper form and technique to prevent injuries and maximize results.

How often should I train with you?

The frequency depends on your goals. For general fitness, 3-4 sessions per week is ideal. For strength or weight loss, I typically recommend 4-5 sessions per week. I'll create a schedule that fits your lifestyle and goals.

Do you provide nutrition advice?

Yes, I provide comprehensive nutrition guidance as part of my training services. We'll work together to develop a balanced diet plan that complements your training and supports your overall health and fitness goals.
