Wine Tasting Notes

Dive deeper into the sensory experience of our wines with detailed tasting notes from our sommeliers.

Estate Pinot Noir - Tasting Notes

Appearance: Deep ruby red with a clear, bright rim.

Aroma: Aromas of fresh black cherry, cranberry, and a hint of clove and dried herbs.

Palate: On the palate, it offers vibrant red fruit flavors, a smooth tannic structure, and a lingering, slightly spicy finish.

Food Pairing: Ideal with grilled salmon, duck, or earthy mushroom dishes.

Old Vines Chardonnay - Tasting Notes

Appearance: A brilliant gold color with a delicate, persistent mousse.

Aroma: Inviting notes of citrus zest, ripe peach, and a subtle vanilla and toasted oak character.

Palate: Rich and creamy on the entry, evolving into bright acidity that balances the full-bodied texture, with a long, buttery finish.

Food Pairing: Complements lobster, scallops, or creamy pastas.
