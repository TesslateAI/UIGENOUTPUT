Frequently Asked Questions

What age range are these storybooks suitable for?

Our storybooks are designed for children aged 2 to 8 years old, with simple language and engaging illustrations suitable for early readers and bedtime stories.

How long does it take to receive my personalized storybook?

Once your customization is complete and approved, it typically takes 5-7 business days for printing and shipping. Delivery times may vary based on location.

Can I add my own text or photo?

Yes! You can add a personal message at the beginning or end of the book, and for the 'Custom Design' option, you can upload a photo of your child to be featured as their character.
