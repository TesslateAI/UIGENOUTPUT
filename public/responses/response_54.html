Our Story

Grace Community Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for decades. We are committed to fostering a deep relationship with God and nurturing a vibrant, loving family.

Founded on the principles of love, service, and worship, we believe in making a tangible difference in the lives of those around us. Our mission is to glorify God by transforming individuals and communities through the power of the Gospel.

We offer a variety of ministries for all ages, from children's programs to youth groups and adult Sunday school, ensuring that every member of your family finds a place to grow in their faith.
