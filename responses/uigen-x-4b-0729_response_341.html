Craftsmanship & Design

Our premium leather jacket is a testament to exceptional craftsmanship. Each piece is meticulously hand-stitched using traditional techniques passed down through generations. The full-grain Italian leather provides an unparalleled texture and develops a beautiful patina over time.

The jacket features a timeless silhouette with a slightly longer back for a flattering fit. The detachable collar and cuffs allow for customization to suit different styles. Inside, premium leather trim accents and lined pockets provide both style and functionality.

Materials & Care

Made from 100% genuine full-grain Italian leather, this jacket is designed to last for decades. The lining is made from breathable Italian silk, providing warmth without bulk. The inner pockets are crafted from water-resistant Cordura fabric for protection of your belongings.

For care instructions:

  • Hand wash with pH-neutral leather cleaner
  • Do not bleach or use strong detergents
  • Air dry away from direct sunlight
  • Reapply leather oil monthly to maintain suppleness
