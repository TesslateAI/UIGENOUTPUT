Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Crafty Kids Club

What age range is Crafty Kids Club for?

Crafty Kids Club is designed for children aged 6-10 years old. Our crafts are carefully developed to be age-appropriate, with varying difficulty levels to suit different ages and skill levels.

How often do I receive a craft kit?

Subscribers receive one craft kit per month. Each kit includes all materials and instructions needed to complete the featured craft. Annual subscribers receive 12 kits per year.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can pause or cancel your subscription at any time. Simply log in to your account and manage your subscription preferences. We don't charge any fees for pausing or canceling.

Are the materials safe for children?

Absolutely! We use only non-toxic, child-safe materials that meet or exceed all safety standards. All kits include safety instructions and age recommendations for each activity.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide! Shipping costs vary by location. Please check our shipping page for specific rates and delivery times for your country.
