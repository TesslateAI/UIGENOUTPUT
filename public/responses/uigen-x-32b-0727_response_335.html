Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photography masterclass

Do I need any prior photography experience?

No prior experience is required! Our masterclass is designed for complete beginners as well as those with some basic knowledge. We start from the fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced techniques.

What equipment do I need?

We recommend a DSLR or mirrorless camera, but smartphones with good cameras are also acceptable for beginners. We provide guidance on choosing equipment based on your budget and goals.

How long do I have access to the course materials?

You'll have lifetime access to all course materials, including future updates. Our community forum and instructor support are available for 1 year after enrollment.

What if I'm not satisfied with the course?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the course content or your progress, you can request a full refund within 30 days of enrollment.
