Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SalesFlow Pro

How long does it take to implement SalesFlow Pro?

Most customers are up and running within 2-4 weeks. Our implementation process includes onboarding, integration with your existing tools, and training for your sales team. We provide dedicated support to ensure a smooth transition.

Can I integrate SalesFlow Pro with my existing CRM?

Absolutely! SalesFlow Pro integrates seamlessly with all major CRM platforms including Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and Zoho. We also offer API access for custom integrations with your existing systems.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 customer support via email and live chat. All plans include access to our knowledge base and video tutorials. Professional and Enterprise plans include dedicated account managers and priority support.

How does the pricing work?

We charge per user per month. Our Starter plan is $49/user/month, Professional is $99/user/month, and Enterprise is $199/user/month. There are no hidden fees, and you can cancel at any time.
