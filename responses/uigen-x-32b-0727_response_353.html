Everything you need to know about transitioning your career with us

No prior coding experience is required! Our programs are designed for complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to advanced concepts. Many of our students come from completely different career backgrounds with no tech experience.

Our full-time programs typically take 10-12 months to complete, while part-time options can take 16-20 months. The exact duration depends on the program you choose and your learning pace. All programs include a 4-week job readiness period at the end.
