The Art of Sourdough

At The Hearth & Loaf, we believe in the ancient art of sourdough fermentation. Our breads are made with a naturally leavened starter, giving them a unique tangy flavor and a chewy, open crumb.

We source our grains locally and bake in small batches, ensuring the highest quality and flavor in every loaf. Our commitment is to wholesome ingredients and traditional methods, bringing you bread that's not just delicious, but nourishing.
