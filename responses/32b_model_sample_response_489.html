Common Questions

Your Questions, Answered

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our virtual bookkeeping and tax optimization services.

What is tax optimization and how does it help freelancers?
Tax optimization involves strategically managing your finances to legally reduce your tax liability. For freelancers, this means identifying eligible deductions, credits, and retirement contributions, helping you keep more of your income and reduce the stress of tax season.
How does CleanSlate handle payroll and self-employment taxes?
We help you estimate and track your quarterly tax payments, ensuring you meet IRS deadlines for self-employment taxes. We also assist with setting up automatic savings for these payments.
Is my financial data secure with CleanSlate Accounting?
Yes, your data is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption and secure platforms to protect your sensitive financial information, ensuring complete confidentiality.
