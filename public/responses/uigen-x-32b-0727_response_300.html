Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cybersecurity services

How long does the vulnerability scan take?

Our automated vulnerability scan typically takes between 1-3 hours to complete, depending on the size and complexity of your network. You'll receive a detailed report within 24 hours after the scan completes.

Is the free vulnerability scan comprehensive?

Yes, our free vulnerability scan covers critical security aspects including open ports, known vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and weak passwords. It provides a solid foundation for understanding your security posture.

How often should I get vulnerability scans?

We recommend vulnerability scans at least quarterly, or after any major system changes. For businesses in highly regulated industries, monthly scans may be appropriate to maintain compliance.

What happens after I request a scan?

After you request a free scan, our system will schedule it within 24 hours. You'll receive an email notification when the scan starts and completes. You'll then get a detailed report with recommendations for improving your security.

Do you offer ongoing protection after the scan?

Absolutely! We offer various subscription plans that include continuous monitoring, regular vulnerability assessments, and priority support to keep your business protected around the clock.
