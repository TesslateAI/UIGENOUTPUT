Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our virtual veterinary services.

TeleVet care is excellent for many conditions, especially when a physical examination isn't required. For complex issues requiring hands-on examination, we'll guide you to the nearest appropriate care facility. Our vets follow the same diagnostic standards as in-person visits.

You'll need a device with a camera and internet connection (smartphone, tablet, or computer). Have your pet's medical history ready, and be prepared to describe symptoms. For some conditions, we may ask you to show specific areas of your pet during the video call.

We offer 24/7 access to veterinary care. Appointments are available immediately during business hours, and within 1-2 hours for urgent needs. Our average wait time is less than 15 minutes for new patients.

Many pet insurance plans now cover telehealth services. We accept most major pet insurance providers. During checkout, you can enter your policy number to check coverage and process claims directly through our system.
