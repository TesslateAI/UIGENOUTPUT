FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the free tier?

The free tier includes access to our entire music library, unlimited playlist creation, and the ability to download up to 5 songs for offline listening. Ad-supported playback is included.

Can I upgrade to a premium account later?

Absolutely! You can easily upgrade to our premium tier at any time to enjoy an ad-free experience, higher audio quality, and more offline downloads.

Is there a time limit on the free tier?

No, our free tier is always available. You can use it indefinitely, though we encourage you to upgrade to premium for the full experience.

What kind of ads will I hear?

Ads on the free tier are short and relevant, designed to be unobtrusive while supporting the service.
