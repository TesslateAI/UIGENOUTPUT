Frequently Asked Questions

Any local individual with a passion for a particular skill can become an instructor, provided they are experienced and passionate about teaching it.

You can offer any skill you're knowledgeable in! From cooking and baking, to crafts, photography, gardening, sewing, and even coding or language learning. The possibilities are endless!

To sign up, simply click the "Sign Up as Instructor" button at the top of the page. Follow the simple steps to create your profile, list your workshops, and connect with your local community.

Instructors receive support through our platform, including guidelines for creating engaging workshops, a community forum for questions, and access to marketing tools to help you reach more learners.
