Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DataGlyph

How does DataGlyph handle large datasets?

DataGlyph is built on a distributed computing framework that can handle datasets with billions of rows. Our platform automatically optimizes queries and uses in-memory processing for fast results.

Can I connect to my existing databases?

Yes! DataGlyph supports connections to all major databases including MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQL Server, Oracle, and BigQuery. We also support API integrations and flat file imports.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. After 14 days, you can choose a plan that fits your needs.

How secure is my data with DataGlyph?

Security is our top priority. We use bank-grade encryption, regular security audits, and comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations. Your data is always your property and we never share it with third parties.
