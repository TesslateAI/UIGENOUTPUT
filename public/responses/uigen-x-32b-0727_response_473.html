About Lumière Festival

Founded in 2005, Lumière Film Festival has grown to become one of the most prestigious cinematic events in the world. We showcase innovative, thought-provoking films from emerging and established filmmakers across the globe.

Our festival is a celebration of storytelling, diversity, and cinematic excellence. Each year, we host over 200 films from more than 50 countries, bringing together audiences and filmmakers for an unforgettable experience.
