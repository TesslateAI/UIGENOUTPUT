Recent Tasting Notes

Château Margaux 2015

★★★★★

Tasted at a friends dinner party. The nose showed incredible depth with blackberries, violets, and a hint of mint. On the palate, the wine was silken and powerful with layers of fruit and mineral complexity. The finish was incredibly long with persistent dark fruit and spice notes.

June 15, 2023 Dinner party

Domaine Leflaive Montrachet 2019

★★★★☆

This was a revelation! The nose was explosive with citrus, hazelnut, and a touch of honey. The palate was rich and powerful yet balanced with incredible acidity that carried the intense flavors. A truly majestic white Burgundy that needs more time to evolve.

May 28, 2023 Solo tasting

Barolo Riserva 2016

★★★★☆

Aged for 3 years in large oak casks. The nose showed tar, rose petals, and dark fruit. The palate was firm and structured with high acidity and tannins, but the fruit was still vibrant. Needs at least 10 more years to fully express its potential.

April 10, 2023 Wine club meeting
