Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FitFlow.

FitFlow stands out with its personalized approach. We don't just offer a standard class; our instructors take the time to understand your unique needs, goals, and level, then design a class specifically for you, followed by detailed, one-on-one feedback to help you improve.

The free trial class is 60 minutes long, allowing you to experience the FitFlow experience fully before committing to a subscription. It's a great way to see how personalized instruction can benefit you.

After your class, you'll receive a personalized video of your session, reviewed by your instructor. They'll highlight key points of your practice, offer form corrections, and provide tips for improvement, all tailored to your specific session.

You'll need a quiet, comfortable space with a yoga mat, a small mirror (optional), and a reliable internet connection for the live video call. A quiet space allows you to fully focus on your practice.
