Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MindfulMoment Journal.

Is there a free version available?

Yes, we offer a free version with basic journaling features. You can access it on our website or through our mobile apps. The free version includes daily journaling prompts, mood tracking, and basic journal entry storage.

How does the premium subscription work?

Our premium subscription is available in three tiers: Starter (free), Mindful ($7/month), and Pro ($12/month). Premium features include personalized journaling prompts, advanced mood tracking, personalized insights, community access, and more. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We take your privacy and security very seriously. All journal entries are encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never share your data with third parties, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Can I use MindfulMoment on multiple devices?

Yes! MindfulMoment is available on iOS, Android, and as a web application. Your journal entries and data are synced across all devices in real-time, so you can access your journal from anywhere.
