Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Our food is made with human-grade ingredients, meaning they meet the same safety standards as food for humans. We also focus on balanced nutrition, tailored recipes, and ethical sourcing.

Feeding frequency depends on your dog's age, breed, and activity level. Puppies typically need more frequent meals, while adults usually thrive on one or two meals a day. Always follow the feeding guidelines on your specific recipe.

We offer a variety of recipes, including options for dogs with specific dietary needs. If your dog has known allergies, we recommend consulting with your veterinarian before making any changes to their diet. Our customer service team is also available to help.

Once you place your order, we prepare and ship your gourmet dog food. Delivery times vary based on your location, but we aim for prompt and reliable service. You can track your order at any time.
