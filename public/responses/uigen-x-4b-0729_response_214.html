FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, our kits are specifically designed for children aged 8-14. Each kit has age-appropriate instructions and difficulty levels. We recommend starting with the Explorer Kit for younger children and progressing to the Master or AI Kit as skills develop.

No prior coding experience is required! Our kits use a visual programming interface that makes coding accessible to beginners. The focus is on building and experimentation rather than complex programming syntax.

Each kit includes all necessary components to build and program robots. This includes robot chassis, sensors, motors, programming cards, building instructions, challenge cards, and a storage box. We also provide free online access to lesson plans and updates.

Our kits are designed to be durable and easy to maintain. We recommend cleaning with a damp cloth for electronics, using soft brushes for sensors, and storing in the included box when not in use. Avoid submerging components in water.
