Our Mission: Making Summer Memories

At Summertime Fun, we believe every child deserves a summer filled with safe, engaging, and educational activities. Our experienced staff, diverse activities, and vibrant environment are designed to foster growth, creativity, and a love for learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

We welcome children aged 4 to 12 years old. Each age group is carefully considered to ensure a safe and engaging environment.

Our camp offers a diverse range of activities including outdoor exploration, creative arts, sports, STEM, music, and more. We ensure there's something for every child's interest!

We maintain a 1:5 staff-to-child ratio, ensuring every child receives personalized attention and a safe, supervised environment throughout the day.

Registration is simple! Visit our "Register" section, select your camp dates, and fill out the form. You can also contact our friendly counselors directly for more information.
