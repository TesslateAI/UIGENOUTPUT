Our Mission

The Museum of Ancient Civilizations is dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich heritage of humanity. Our collection spans over 5,000 years of history, featuring artifacts from Egypt, Mesopotamia, Greece, and beyond.

Our audio tour provides an immersive experience, bringing each artifact to life with expert commentary and ambient soundscapes.

History & Heritage

Founded in 1923

Established by renowned archaeologist Dr. Eleanor Vance, our museum was the first to implement systematic artifact preservation techniques.

UNESCO Recognition

Designated a World Heritage Site in 1987 for our unparalleled collection of ancient artifacts and conservation efforts.

Tutankhamun's Golden Mask

3:45 / 7:22

Exhibit Details

This exquisite gold mask, crafted for the young pharaoh Tutankhamun, is one of the most iconic artifacts of ancient Egypt. Made of solid gold and inlaid with lapis lazuli, the mask weighs over 11 kilograms and features intricate details that symbolize the pharaoh's divine status. Discovered in 1922 by Howard Carter, it remains one of the most significant finds from the Valley of the Kings.
