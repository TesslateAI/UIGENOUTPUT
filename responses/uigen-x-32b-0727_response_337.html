Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nova CRM

How long does the free trial last?

Our free trial lasts for 14 days. During this period, you'll have full access to all features of the Professional plan. No credit card is required to start your trial.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a limit to the number of users?

The Starter plan includes up to 5 users, Professional includes up to 20 users, and Enterprise offers unlimited users. You can add or remove users at any time with no additional cost per user.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer email support for all customers. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support with faster response times. We also have an extensive knowledge base and video tutorials.
