Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the course

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience is required! Our course is designed for beginners as well as intermediate producers looking to refine their skills. We start from the fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced techniques.

How long do I have access to the course?

You get lifetime access to all course materials, including future updates and additions. Once you enroll, you can learn at your own pace, whenever and wherever you want.

What if I'm not satisfied with the course?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with the course for any reason, just let us know within 30 days of enrollment and we'll refund your payment in full.

Will I get feedback on my projects?

Yes! All Pro and VIP students receive personalized feedback from our instructors. Pro students get 4 sessions, while VIP students get unlimited access to mentoring and project reviews.

What software do I need?

We teach using industry-standard DAWs like Ableton Live, Logic Pro, and FL Studio. You can use any DAW you prefer. We provide tutorials for multiple platforms.
