Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our financial planning services

Is my free consultation confidential?

Absolutely. Your financial information is completely confidential. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit permission.

How long does a typical financial review take?

Our initial consultation is 30 minutes, followed by a 60-minute review of your financial documents. Additional time may be needed depending on your specific needs.

What if I don't like the recommended plan?

We believe in collaborative planning. If you have concerns about our recommendations, we'll work together to adjust the plan to better suit your preferences.

What fees do you charge?

We offer a free initial consultation. Our fees are based on a percentage of your assets under management, typically starting at 1%. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Can I start with just a consultation?

Yes! Our free consultation allows us to understand your needs before committing to ongoing services. Many clients start with just a consultation and then choose additional services.
