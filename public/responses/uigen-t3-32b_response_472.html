Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your common questions about solar energy and our audit process.

What is a free energy audit?

A free energy audit is a comprehensive assessment of your home's energy usage and potential for solar power. We analyze your utility bills, roof condition, and energy habits to provide a personalized solar plan.

How long does the audit take?

The audit typically takes about 30-45 minutes, either in-person or via a virtual consultation, depending on your preference and location.

Is there any cost for the audit?

No, our energy audit is completely free and no-obligation. We simply want to help you understand your energy potential.

What happens after the audit?

After the audit, you'll receive a detailed report outlining your home's solar potential, estimated savings, and a customized solar plan. You can then decide if you'd like to proceed with installation.

Do I need to be home for the audit?

Yes, we recommend being home during the audit so we can ask questions and review your energy usage directly with you. For virtual audits, we'll need access to your utility bills and basic home information.
