Our Signature Flavor Blends

Each GourmetGrill Kit is a curated collection of unique spice rubs and sauces, designed to transform your grilling experience.

Smoky Chili Rub

A bold blend of smoked paprika, cumin, and dark chili for a deep, rich flavor.

  • Perfect for ribs, brisket, or chicken.
  • Features a subtle hint of cocoa for depth.

Zesty Lime Crema

A refreshing, tangy sauce with fresh lime, cilantro, and a touch of honey.

  • Ideal for tacos, grilled vegetables, or seafood.
  • Light and vibrant, perfect for summer grilling.

Spicy Garlic Salt

A powerful combination of roasted garlic, cayenne pepper, and sea salt.

  • Great for steaks, potatoes, or as a seasoning for popcorn.
  • Simple yet incredibly flavorful.

Sweet BBQ Sauce

A classic, sticky sauce with a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess.

  • Perfect for chicken wings, pork, or as a dipping sauce.
  • Infused with apple cider vinegar and brown sugar.

Herb Marinade

A fresh, aromatic blend of rosemary, thyme, and garlic for tender meats.

  • Excellent for chicken, lamb, or grilled vegetables.
  • Features a touch of olive oil for richness.

Spicy Harissa Rub

A fiery North African blend with harissa paste, coriander, and cumin.

  • Ideal for lamb, fish, or roasted vegetables.
  • Brings a unique, smoky heat.
