Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our online physiotherapy services.

Our online physiotherapy sessions are conducted via secure video call. During your appointment, our physiotherapist will assess your injury, demonstrate exercises, and provide personalized guidance. You'll receive a customized treatment plan to follow between sessions.

Yes, for many types of injuries, online physiotherapy can be just as effective as in-person sessions. Our physiotherapists are trained to assess and treat a wide range of conditions through virtual consultations. For complex cases requiring hands-on techniques, we may recommend in-person care or collaborate with local providers.

For your first session, you'll need a stable internet connection, a device with a camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone), and a private space where you can move comfortably. Wear clothing that allows the physiotherapist to see the injured area. You'll also need to complete a short medical history form before your appointment.

The timeline for recovery varies depending on the type and severity of your injury. Many patients report feeling relief within the first few sessions. Our physiotherapists will provide realistic expectations during your initial assessment and adjust your treatment plan as you progress.
