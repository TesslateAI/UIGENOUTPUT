Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of therapy does HealHub offer?

We offer a range of therapeutic modalities, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), and trauma-informed care, tailored to individual needs.

How secure is my information?

Your privacy and confidentiality are paramount. We use industry-standard encryption and adhere to strict data protection policies to ensure your information is secure.

What devices can I use for therapy sessions?

You can attend sessions via video call (e.g., Zoom, Google Meet), audio call, or chat, using any device with a stable internet connection, including desktops, laptops, tablets, or smartphones.
