Practical Tips to Lower Your Carbon Footprint

Simple actions that make a big difference for the environment

Switch to Renewable Energy

Install solar panels or choose a renewable energy provider. On average, switching to solar can reduce your electricity-related carbon footprint by 80%.

Drive Less, Ride More

Use public transportation, carpool, bike, or walk for your daily commute. These actions can significantly reduce transportation-related emissions.

Reduce Food Waste

Plan meals, store food properly, and compost organic waste. Food waste accounts for nearly 20% of greenhouse gas emissions in the US.

Conserve Water

Fix leaks, install low-flow fixtures, and use water-saving appliances. Water conservation reduces energy consumption for water treatment and distribution.
