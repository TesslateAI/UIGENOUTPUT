Our Story

A Taste of the World

For over two decades, we've been curating the world's most authentic culinary treasures

Our Journey Begins

Founded in 1998 by culinary enthusiasts Marco and Elena Rossi, Savory Journeys began as a passion project to share authentic flavors from around the world. What started as a small import shop in Florence has grown into an international gourmet brand.

We partner directly with artisans, farmers, and producers in over 30 countries to bring you products made with traditional methods passed down through generations.
