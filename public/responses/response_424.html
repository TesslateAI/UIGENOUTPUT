Master Your Brew

Pour-Over Coffee

This method allows for precise control over water temperature and flow rate, resulting in a clean, bright cup. Perfect for highlighting the nuanced flavors of single-origin beans.

  • Use a V60 or Hario dripper.
  • Bloom the coffee with hot water.
  • Pour water in slow, circular motions.

French Press

A full-immersion brewing method that produces a rich, full-bodied coffee with a heavier mouthfeel. It's excellent for darker roasts and robust beans.

  • Use coarse ground coffee.
  • Steep for 4-5 minutes.
  • Press the plunger down slowly.
