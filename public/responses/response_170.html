What is MindfulMakers?

MindfulMakers Crafts is a curated subscription box for adults, bringing you unique, hand-picked crafting projects delivered right to your door. Each box includes all the materials you need to complete a beautiful and relaxing project, along with easy-to-follow instructions.

We believe in the transformative power of hands-on creation as a form of self-care and stress relief. Our mission is to provide a convenient, accessible, and deeply fulfilling way to incorporate mindfulness into your daily life through the joy of crafting.

Community Connection

Personal Growth

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of projects will I receive?

We feature a diverse range of projects, including but not limited to: knitting, crocheting, paper crafts (like origami and scrapbooking), jewelry making, plant care (terrariums), and mindfulness activities. Each project is chosen for its therapeutic value and ease of completion.

Do I need any prior crafting experience?

No prior experience is necessary! Our boxes are designed for adults of all skill levels. We provide clear, step-by-step instructions and all the necessary materials for each project.

How often are the boxes shipped?

Boxes are shipped monthly. Quarterly and yearly subscriptions receive boxes on a monthly basis as well, with the projects spread out over the chosen period.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Please note that cancellations are effective for future shipments, not retroactive for boxes already ordered.
