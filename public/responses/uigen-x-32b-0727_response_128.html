Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GameCraft Studio

Do I need programming experience to use GameCraft?

Not necessarily! GameCraft's visual editor allows you to create games without any coding knowledge. However, we also support scripting for more advanced functionality if you have programming experience.

Can I export my games to multiple platforms?

Yes! GameCraft supports exporting to Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, and web platforms. Our cross-platform tools make it easy to deploy your game to all major platforms with a single click.

Is the asset library free to use?

We offer both free and premium assets. Our starter plan includes access to 500 free assets, while our Pro and Enterprise plans provide access to our full library of 5,000+ assets and premium content.

Can I collaborate with my team in real-time?

Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes real-time collaboration tools, version control, and cloud storage so your team can work together seamlessly on the same project.
