About Genealogy Explorer

Genealogy Explorer was founded in 2010 with a mission to make family history research accessible to everyone. Our team of professional genealogists, archivists, and historians work together to provide accurate and engaging family history resources.

We believe that understanding your past is essential to building your future. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with centuries of historical knowledge to help you discover who you are and where you come from.
