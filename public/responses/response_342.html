Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and how it works.

What languages can I learn?

You can learn any language that another community member is teaching. Our platform supports a wide variety of languages, from major global languages like English, Spanish, and Mandarin to less commonly taught ones.

Is it free to use?

Yes! Our core language exchange features are completely free. You can connect with partners, chat, and video call without any cost. We offer premium features for those who want to enhance their experience.

How do I find a good language partner?

Our search filters allow you to look for partners by language, proficiency level, interests, and more. You can also browse profiles and send messages to potential partners to see if you're a good fit.

What if I don't find a partner quickly?

Don't worry! Our community is growing, and new users join every day. You can also participate in our community forums and events to meet more potential partners.
