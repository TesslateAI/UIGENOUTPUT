The Celestial Shard

RARE

Discovery

The Celestial Shard was unearthed in the remote ruins of Astralis, a forgotten city from the ancient era of the Astral Empire. It was believed to hold remnants of a powerful cosmic force, a relic of the first stars.

History of Ownership

The shard passed through the hands of many, from a humble blacksmith to a revered astronomer, and finally to a secretive order of astral navigators. Each claimed it had a unique power, though none could replicate its true essence.

Its Power

Legend tells of its ability to reveal hidden paths through the astral plane, granting its wielder unparalleled vision. It is said to respond only to those with the purest intent and the deepest understanding of the cosmos.

Key Properties

Type: Cosmic Relic
Weight: 0.5 lbs (227g)
Material: Astral Quartz & Void Iron
Resonance: Astral (Requires Astral Focus)
Condition: Perfect (No visible damage)
Energy Signature: Stable, low fluctuation
