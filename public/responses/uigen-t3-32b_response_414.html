Simple. Smart. Sustainable.

Get from A to B with ease and a lighter footprint. Our three-step process makes riding an e-scooter effortless.

2. Unlock & Ride

Scan the QR code or enter the PIN to unlock your scooter. Ride off with confidence.

3. Park & Share

Park your scooter responsibly. Your ride helps others, and the planet.

A Greener Future Starts Here

EcoRide is committed to reducing urban congestion and carbon emissions. By choosing an e-scooter, you're contributing to a cleaner, quieter, and more sustainable city.

  • Reduces car trips and traffic congestion.
  • Zero tailpipe emissions, lowering air pollution.
  • Promotes active lifestyles and short-distance travel.
  • Supports the development of eco-friendly infrastructure.

To unlock a scooter, simply scan the QR code on the scooter's handlebars using the EcoRide app, or enter the unique PIN displayed on the app. The scooter will emit a sound and light up when unlocked.

Our pricing is transparent and fair. You pay a small unlock fee, plus a per-minute rate while the scooter is active. Check our app for the exact rates in your area.

Always park your scooter responsibly in designated parking zones, away from pedestrian paths, bike lanes, and private property. Improper parking may result in a fee.

Yes, you must be at least 18 years old to ride an EcoRide scooter. We also require a valid driver's license for account verification.
