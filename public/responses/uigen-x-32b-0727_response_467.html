Our Story

Founded in 1923, Luxury Haven has been a sanctuary for travelers seeking authentic experiences and timeless elegance. What began as a modest family-run inn has evolved into a celebrated boutique hotel, preserving its historic charm while embracing modern comforts.

Our commitment to exceptional hospitality remains unchanged. Each room tells a story, each meal is a celebration of local flavors, and every moment is crafted to create unforgettable memories.
