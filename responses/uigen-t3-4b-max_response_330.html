Frequently Asked Questions

What is ESG investing?

ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investing involves evaluating companies based on these three factors, beyond just financial performance, to make more informed investment decisions that align with ethical values.

Will I lose money investing sustainably?

No, not necessarily. In fact, many studies show that ESG-aligned portfolios can perform as well as, or even outperform, traditional portfolios over the long term. The key is to integrate ESG factors effectively.

Do I need a lot of money to start?

Absolutely not! There are numerous low-cost ESG ETFs and mutual funds, as well as direct investments in ESG-aligned companies, that make sustainable investing accessible to anyone, regardless of their initial capital.
