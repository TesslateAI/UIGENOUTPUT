Your Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about bespoke tailoring and our online process.

The bespoke process typically takes 6-8 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery, depending on the complexity and current workload. We ensure you are kept informed at every stage.

We offer a wide selection of premium fabrics from renowned mills such as Loro Piana, Scabal, and Zegna, including fine wools, cashmere, and silk blends. Our tailors can guide you through the best options for your desired look and feel.

We provide detailed measurement guides and video tutorials to help you take accurate measurements at home. For an even more precise fit, we can also arrange for a professional tailor to visit your location for direct measurements.
