Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for a mentor?

Early-career professionals, including recent graduates, those transitioning to new roles, or looking to accelerate their current career, are eligible.

What kind of mentorship is offered?

It's a personalized, one-on-one mentorship with a highly qualified, experienced professional from your industry or a related field, focusing on career growth and strategic planning.

How long does the mentorship last?

The program typically lasts 6-12 months, with the exact duration and frequency of meetings based on your needs and your mentor's availability.

Is there a commitment required?

Yes, we encourage a commitment to the process, including regular check-ins and active participation in the mentorship journey.
