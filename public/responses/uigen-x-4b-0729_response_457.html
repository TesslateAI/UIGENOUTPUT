Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our booking system

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment by logging into your account and selecting the appointment you wish to change. If you need to cancel within 24 hours of your appointment, a full refund will be issued. For rescheduling, we'll attempt to move you to the next available slot at no additional cost.

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also support payments through PayPal and Apple Pay. For certain services, we may require a deposit to secure your appointment. Full payment is due at the time of booking.

Yes, you can book multiple appointments in one day. When booking, simply select the "Add Another Appointment" option and choose your second service and time slot. Please note that each appointment requires its own booking and payment. We recommend confirming with our support team if you need assistance with multiple bookings.

For medical appointments, we recommend arriving at least 15 minutes before your scheduled time. For non-urgent services, please arrive 10 minutes before your appointment. We ask that you arrive on time to respect the availability of our staff and other customers. If you are running late, please call our office immediately to inform us.
