Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions? We're Here to Help

What is CalmSpace?

CalmSpace is a mental wellness app designed to provide guided meditations, mindfulness exercises, and calming sounds to help you reduce stress, improve focus, and cultivate inner peace.

Is CalmSpace free to use?

CalmSpace offers a range of free features, including a selection of guided meditations and sounds. For full access to all content, including premium meditations and personalized plans, a subscription is available.

How often should I use CalmSpace?

We recommend starting with 5-10 minutes of guided meditation daily. Consistency is key, so find a time that fits your schedule and make it a part of your self-care routine.

Can I track my progress with CalmSpace?

Yes, CalmSpace allows you to track your meditation sessions, progress over time, and receive insights to help you understand your wellness journey. This feature is available in the premium plan.
