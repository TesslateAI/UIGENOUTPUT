Course Structure (12 Weeks)

Week 1-2: Foundations of Photography

  • Understanding the camera's basics
  • Exposure triangle: Aperture, Shutter Speed, ISO
  • Lighting 101: Natural vs. Artificial

Week 3-4: Compositional Techniques

  • Golden Ratio & Leading Lines
  • Rule of Thirds & Symmetry
  • Depth of Field & Portraits

Week 5-6: Post-Processing Essentials

  • Lightroom 101: Adjustments & Exposure
  • Snapseed: Quick Enhancements
  • Crop & Straighten for Better Results

Week 7-8: Advanced Lighting & Techniques

  • Golden Hour & Blue Hour
  • Reflections & Refractions
  • Macro Photography & Close-ups

Week 9-10: Creative Storytelling & Style

  • Documentary & Travel Photography
  • Portraiture: Capturing Emotions
  • Developing Your Unique Visual Identity

Week 11-12: Final Project & Mastery

  • Mastering your final project
  • Revisions & Final Output
  • Presenting Your Work
