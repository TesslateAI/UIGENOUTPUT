How PureFlow Works

Our 4-stage filtration process ensures your water is as pure as nature intended

1
2
3
4

Stage 1: Sediment Filter

Removes large particles like sand, silt, and rust to protect the system and improve water clarity.

Stage 2: Carbon Block Filter

Eliminates chlorine, bad tastes, odors, and volatile organic compounds (VOCs) for great-tasting water.

Stage 3: Reverse Osmosis

Removes dissolved solids, heavy metals, and other contaminants at the molecular level.

Stage 4: Post-Carbon Filter

Polishes the water for the purest taste by removing any remaining impurities and improving flavor.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PureFlow water filters

How often do I need to replace the filters?

PureFlow filters are designed to last 6-12 months depending on water quality and usage. We provide filter replacement reminders to ensure optimal performance.

What types of contaminants does PureFlow remove?

Our system removes 99.9% of chlorine, lead, mercury, bacteria, cysts, sediment, and over 1,000 other contaminants. It's certified by NSF International for water safety.

How much does the system cost?

PureFlow systems start at $299 with a free installation. We offer flexible payment options including a monthly payment plan. The system pays for itself in bottled water savings within 6-12 months.

Can I install it myself?

While the system is designed for easy installation, we recommend professional installation to ensure optimal performance. Our technicians will handle everything from setup to testing.
