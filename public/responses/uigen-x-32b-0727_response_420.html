Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our business loans

How quickly can I get a business loan?

Most applications are processed instantly, with funds deposited in your account within 24 hours. In rare cases where additional verification is needed, we typically complete the process within 48 hours.

What are the eligibility requirements?

To qualify, you'll need:

  • A business operating for at least 6 months
  • A personal credit score of 600 or higher
  • A bank account in your business name
  • Proof of income or business financials

Are there any fees associated with the loan?

We have no application fees, processing fees, or prepayment penalties. Our fees are transparent and clearly outlined in your loan agreement. The only cost is the interest charged on the principal amount.

Can I repay the loan early?

Yes, you can repay your loan at any time without any prepayment penalties. Early repayment can help you save on interest costs.
