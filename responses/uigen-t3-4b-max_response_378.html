Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our workshops.

Our workshops are designed for children aged 8 to 14, with curriculum tailored to different skill levels within that range.

We cover a variety of coding concepts depending on the workshop. Our Introduction to Coding workshop uses visual block-based programming (e.g., Scratch), while our Web Development and Mobile App workshops introduce HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All are engaging and accessible for kids.

Our workshops are interactive and hands-on, with a mix of guided activities, independent coding projects, and group challenges. Instructors provide support and encouragement throughout.

Currently, we offer in-person workshops at our local community centers. We are continuously exploring online options to provide flexibility for more families. Please check our website for the latest updates.
