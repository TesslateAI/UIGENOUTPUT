Our Materials

We source only the most sustainable and ethical materials for our products.

Organic Cotton

Grown without harmful pesticides, softer on skin and the environment.

Recycled Polyester

Made from post-consumer plastic bottles, reducing ocean waste.

Hemp Fabric

Durable, fast-growing crop that requires minimal water and no pesticides.

Natural Dyes

Plant-based dyes that are non-toxic and biodegradable.
