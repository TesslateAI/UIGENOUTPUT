Property Description

This stunning modern villa offers luxury living at its finest. Situated on a private lot in the prestigious Beverly Hills neighborhood, this property features floor-to-ceiling windows, panoramic ocean views, and a state-of-the-art home entertainment system.

The open-concept living areas are perfect for entertaining, while the gourmet kitchen is equipped with top-of-the-line appliances and custom cabinetry. The primary suite features a spa-like bathroom with a soaking tub and walk-in closet.

Outdoor living is seamless with a large infinity pool, outdoor kitchen, and fire pit. The professionally landscaped grounds provide a serene retreat.
