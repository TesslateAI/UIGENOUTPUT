Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the workshop?

Each workshop includes expert-led live sessions, personalized feedback, writing exercises, and a shared resource library. We also offer optional one-on-one coaching for an additional fee.

What kind of writing will I focus on?

SkillUp workshops are designed for a wide range of genres, including fiction, non-fiction, memoir, and short stories. Our instructors will guide you through the specific aspects relevant to your chosen genre.

How do I access the workshop?

Once you register, you'll gain immediate access to the virtual platform. All sessions, materials, and discussions will take place there. We also provide a downloadable resource guide.
