Frequently Asked Questions

What VR headsets do you offer?

We offer the latest generation VR headsets, including models from Meta (Quest 3), PlayStation VR 2, and HTC Vive Pro. All gear is sanitized and ready for your comfort.

Do I need prior VR experience?

No prior VR experience is necessary! Our staff will provide a quick orientation to get you started. We cater to all skill levels.

What games are available?

Our library includes a wide variety of games, from thrilling adventures like "Half-Life: Alyx" to social experiences like "VRChat" and competitive titles like "Beat Saber." Check our website for the full list.

What are your age restrictions?

Our minimum age for individual sessions is 10 years old. For younger children, we offer family-friendly sessions with parental supervision.
