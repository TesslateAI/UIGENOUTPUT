Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about PawPrint.

PawPrint offers highly accurate GPS tracking, typically within 5-10 meters in open areas. Accuracy can vary based on environmental factors like buildings or dense foliage.

With normal usage, PawPrint's battery lasts up to 14 days on a single charge. Battery life may vary depending on tracking frequency and environmental conditions.
