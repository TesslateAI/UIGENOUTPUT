Our Story

Learn about our church's history and mission

A Community of Believers

Founded in 1952, Saints Community Church has grown from a small gathering of 20 people to a thriving congregation of over 500 families. Our mission remains unchanged: to worship God, serve our community, and nurture spiritual growth.

We believe in the Great Commission - going into all the world to make disciples of Jesus Christ. Through our outreach programs, Bible studies, and community service, we strive to live out our faith in practical ways.
