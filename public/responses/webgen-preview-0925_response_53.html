Frequently Asked Questions

How does real‑time arrival information work?

Our system pulls live data from transit agencies via API. When a bus or train is approaching, the app updates the estimated arrival time instantly.

Can I save multiple favorite routes?

Yes! After planning a route, tap the star icon to add it to your favorites. You can view them from the dashboard anytime.

Is the app free?

The core scheduling and route‑planning features are completely free. Premium features like offline maps are available for a small subscription.
