Our Story

Founded in 1985, Grace & Mercy Church has been a beacon of hope and community for over 35 years.

Church Building Image

Rooted in Love, Growing in Grace

Grace & Mercy Church began as a small group of believers meeting in a community center. Today, we've grown into a vibrant community of over 1,500 members, yet we maintain our commitment to personal connection and spiritual growth.

Our mission is to love God, love people, and make disciples who transform the world. We believe that every person matters to God and that through Jesus Christ, we can experience true freedom and purpose.
