Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service.

What is the process for a free meet-and-greet?

It's simple! You can schedule a free, no-obligation meeting online or via our contact form. This allows us to get to know your dog and you, and for you to get to know us.

Are your dog walkers certified?

Yes, absolutely! All of our certified dog walkers have undergone rigorous vetting, training, and are fully insured to ensure the highest standard of care for your pet.

What if my dog is nervous or has special needs?

We understand every dog is unique. When you book your free meet-and-greet, you can share any specific needs or concerns (e.g., anxiety, medical conditions). We will then recommend the best possible walker for your pet.

How do you ensure safety?

Safety is our top priority. We use secure, well-maintained walking routes, and our walkers are always trained to handle various situations. We also provide detailed walk reports to give you peace of mind.
