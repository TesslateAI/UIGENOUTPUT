Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coaching services and process.

Our coaching process is structured 8-week program with weekly sessions. The first session is an assessment to understand your goals and challenges. Subsequent sessions focus on specific strategies, progress tracking, and accountability. We also provide resources and homework to support your growth between sessions.

Our coaching packages start at $120 per session (60 minutes). We also offer flexible monthly packages that include 4-5 sessions at a discounted rate. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the results.

We believe in flexibility and understanding. If you need to miss a session, please inform us at least 24 hours in advance. We'll reschedule at your convenience. Consistent attendance is important for progress, but we prioritize your personal circumstances.

Clients typically report significant improvements in mindset, self-awareness, and personal habits within 4-6 weeks. Many experience increased confidence, better relationships, career advancement, and improved overall well-being. The exact results depend on your commitment and the specific goals you work on.
