Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our coaching

What makes your coaching different?

Our coaching integrates mind, body, and spirit using evidence-based practices combined with personalized guidance. We focus on sustainable change rather than quick fixes, helping you build lasting wellness habits.

Do you offer virtual coaching?

Yes, all our coaching sessions are virtual and can be conducted via secure video call. We also offer in-person sessions at our wellness center.

What if I'm not satisfied with my session?

We believe in your satisfaction. If you're not completely happy with your coaching experience, we'll work together to find solutions or provide a full refund.

How often should I meet with you?

We typically meet weekly for 60-minute sessions. The frequency can be adjusted based on your progress and specific needs.

Do you provide resources outside sessions?

Absolutely! We provide personalized resources, guided meditations, and tools to support your progress between sessions.
