Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Heritage Hues natural dyes eco-friendly?

Our natural dyes are derived from plant, fruit, and mineral sources, avoiding synthetic chemicals. They are biodegradable, non-toxic, and often cruelty-free, making them a sustainable choice for artists.

Are the dyes suitable for all fabric types?

While most natural dyes work best on natural fibers like cotton, linen, and hemp, some kits include additional materials or techniques to achieve vibrant results on synthetic or blended fabrics. Always follow the kit's instructions for best results.

How long do the natural dyes last?

When properly stored and used, the natural dyes in Heritage Hues can last for several months, sometimes even years, depending on the specific dye and storage conditions. Always check the kit's instructions for optimal shelf life.

Where can I find more information or support?

Our website has comprehensive tutorials, a FAQ section, and a dedicated support email. You can also connect with us on social media for tips, tricks, and community support.
