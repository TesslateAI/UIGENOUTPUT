Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

FitKids Fun Camps are designed for children aged 5 to 12 years old.

We maintain a low camper-to-staff ratio, typically 8:1 for younger campers and 10:1 for older campers, to ensure individualized attention and safety.

Each program has a specific list, but generally, bring a full water bottle, a lunch, a snack, sunscreen, a hat, and appropriate clothing for physical activity. We'll send you a detailed list when you register.

We provide morning and afternoon snacks. Campers are required to bring their own lunch from home.
