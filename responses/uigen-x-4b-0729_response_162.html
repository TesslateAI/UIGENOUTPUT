Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our language tutoring service.

How do I book my first lesson?

Booking your first lesson is simple! Browse our available tutors, select your preferred language and date/time, and complete the payment process. You'll receive a confirmation email with a link to join your first lesson.

What languages do you tutor?

We offer tutoring in Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Mandarin Chinese, and more. Our instructors specialize in both Romance and Germanic languages, with native speakers for all options.

Can I choose my tutor?

Absolutely! We match you with a tutor based on your learning style, goals, and availability. You can also request specific tutors if they're available in your language preference.

What if I need to cancel a lesson?

We require 24 hours notice for lesson cancellations. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a $25 fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.
