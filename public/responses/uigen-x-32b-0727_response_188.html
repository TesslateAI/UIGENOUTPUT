Premium Sound Experience

The SonicPro Wireless Headphones deliver an unparalleled audio experience with custom-tuned 40mm drivers that produce rich, detailed sound across the entire frequency range. Whether you're listening to music, watching movies, or making calls, you'll enjoy crystal-clear audio.

Our advanced Active Noise Cancellation technology uses six microphones to continuously monitor and block external noise, allowing you to immerse yourself in your audio without distractions. The adaptive EQ automatically tunes music to the shape of your ear for consistent, rich sound.

Extended Battery Life

30 hours of battery life with ANC enabled, or 40 hours without. Quick charge provides 5 hours of playback with just 10 minutes of charging.

Seamless Connectivity

Bluetooth 5.2 with multipoint connectivity allows you to connect to two devices simultaneously. Low latency mode ensures perfect sync for gaming and video.
