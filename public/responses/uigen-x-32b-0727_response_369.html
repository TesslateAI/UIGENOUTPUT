About the Event

Join thousands of runners and walkers as we come together for a cause greater than ourselves.

Our Mission

To raise funds for local food banks and support communities in need. Every registration helps us make a difference.

The Run

A scenic 5K route through the city park with water stations, medical support, and a post-race celebration.

Prizes & Awards

Exciting prizes for top runners, participation medals for all, and special awards for team spirit.

The Run Route

A beautiful 5K route through the heart of our city park with scenic views and easy navigation.
