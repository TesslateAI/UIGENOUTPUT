Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required?

We typically require business license, tax returns, bank statements, and a recent business bank account statement. Details will be provided during the application process.

How quickly can I get approved?

In many cases, we can provide a quick decision within 24-48 hours after submitting your completed application. Some cases may take up to 5 business days.

Are there any fees?

We offer competitive interest rates, and there are no hidden fees for the application process itself. However, interest will apply to the loan amount.

What are the loan terms?

Loan terms vary depending on the amount and type of loan. We offer a range of terms, from 6 months to 5 years, to suit different business needs.
