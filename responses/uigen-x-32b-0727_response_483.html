Gameplay Overview

1

Setup

Players choose unique characters and assemble the modular board. Place enemy forces according to difficulty level.

2

Resource Gathering

Collect resources from your territories to build structures, train units, and research technologies.

3

Combat

Defend your lands and attack enemy positions using your character's unique abilities and tactics.

4

Victory

Work together to complete the final boss encounter and protect the kingdom from destruction.
