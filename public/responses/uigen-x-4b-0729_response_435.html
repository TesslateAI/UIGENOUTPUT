Learning Tips

Active Practice

Flashcards work best when you actively engage with them. Try explaining the meaning aloud or using them in a sentence.

Spaced Repetition

Review flashcards at increasing intervals to help your brain retain the information long-term.

Pronunciation Practice

Listen to native speaker recordings to improve your pronunciation and get feedback.

Set Goals

Set daily targets and track your progress to stay motivated and see your improvement.
