Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Craft Beer Club.

What is the Craft Beer Club?

The Craft Beer Club is a monthly subscription service that delivers a curated selection of premium craft beers directly to your door. Each month, you'll receive a unique lineup of beers from top breweries around the world.

How often will I receive beers?

Members receive a new selection of craft beers every month. You can pause or cancel your membership at any time.

What if I don't like a beer in my box?

We're confident you'll love our selections, but if you ever receive a beer you don't enjoy, simply let us know, and we'll do our best to address your concerns or adjust future selections.

Can I skip a month?

Yes, you can easily skip a month through your account dashboard. Just log in and manage your preferences.
