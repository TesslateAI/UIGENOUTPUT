Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free introductory course really free?

Yes, the introductory course is completely free! We offer it to help you assess if coding is the right path for you before committing to a full bootcamp.

Do I need any prior coding experience?

No prior coding experience is required! Our program is designed for complete beginners, and our instructors will guide you every step of the way.

What kind of job placement support do you offer?

We offer comprehensive job placement support, including personalized career coaching, resume and LinkedIn profile reviews, mock interviews, and direct introductions to potential employers.

How long does the full bootcamp take?

Our full-time bootcamps typically last 12-16 weeks, while our part-time options range from 24-32 weeks, allowing you to learn at a pace that fits your lifestyle.
