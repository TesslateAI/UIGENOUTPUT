Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How does the delivery process work?

Once you select a plan, we'll deliver your dog's meals directly to your door every week. Our meals are frozen for freshness and arrive with ice packs to keep them cool during transit.

Can I customize my dog's meals?

Absolutely! When you sign up, we'll ask about your dog's preferences, allergies, and dietary needs to create a personalized meal plan. You can also adjust your plan at any time.

What if my dog doesn't like a particular recipe?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If your dog doesn't love a recipe, simply let us know and we'll replace it with another option at no extra cost.

How do I pause or cancel my subscription?

You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. We'll notify you before each delivery so you can make changes as needed.
