About This Campaign

In rural communities across Africa, many families walk miles each day to collect water from contaminated sources. This campaign aims to install sustainable water wells in three remote villages, providing clean drinking water to 500 families.

Each well will be maintained by local community members with training provided by our partners. We're committed to ensuring long-term sustainability and impact.
